# Tryout rubric — 16U

> Eight-row, five-column observable rubric for evaluating 16U club volleyball tryouts. Column 3 is the 16s standard ([[16s]] promotion criteria, mirrored). Column 5 is 17s-ready (early advanced markers — flag for 17U-or-above placement). Use alongside [[age-lens-hs]] (parent context) and [[recruiting]] (16s is the recruiting auto-evaluation year).

## Purpose

The 16U tryout evaluates against the same milestones the 16s age-guide says a player must demonstrate to graduate to 17s. Eight skill areas, five-point scale, observable cells. Specialization-phase year — every athlete should arrive with a position identity (libero / OH / MB / S / Opp), at least one HS varsity season behind her, and a settled offensive system. The rubric does the recruiting-relevant first-pass: which athletes are 16s-standard, which are 17s-ready, and which need targeted development to hold a roster spot. Pair with the matched [[16s]] age-guide for the underlying milestone rationale.

## Evaluation criteria

| Skill | 1: not yet | 2: occasional | 3: 16s standard | 4: above 16s | 5: 17s-ready |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Passing** | Pass-grade <1.5 in 6v6; mishandles routine floats | Pass-grade 1.5-1.8; misses libero-split read on jump-floats | Pass-grade ~2.0; libero-split serviceable [[serve-receive-libero-split]]; calls early/loud | Pass-grade 2.2+; reads server toss-and-shoulder pre-contact | Pass-grade 2.4+ vs. varsity jump-topspin; OH-2 backup-libero capable |
| **Setting** | Stand-sets only; middle quick fails | Jump-sets sometimes; middle quick fails on imperfect pass | Jump-sets reliably; runs Tempo 1/2/3 on graded passes; basic OOS hittable 50%+ | Disguised backsets; OOS hittable 70%+; calls audibles | OOS hittable 80%+; reads block before set; in-rotation tempo adjustment |
| **Hitting** | One shot only; approach inconsistent; misses approach reps | Two shots called pre-swing; back-row attempt with low success | 4-step approach mature; 3+ shots; back-row 30%+ kill rate vs. single block | Closes-heel consistently; reads block; tip/roll/cut as live tools | Back-row 40%+ vs. double; one-foot landing recovery; recruiting-grade approach-touch |
| **Blocking** | Square only; tooling room; runs back to land | Swing-block emerging; inconsistent timing; some controlled landings | Swing-block footwork mature [[blocking-swing]]; bunch-read awareness; step-back default | Reads hitter pre-set; commit-block timing; outside-foot-high seam-close | Reads setter eyes; in-rotation block-scheme calls; right-side cross-court block |
| **Serving** | Float only; high error rate; targets break under pressure | Jump-float emerging; targets ~70%; no jump-topspin attempt | Float and jump-float live; targets hold under 10-zone pressure; jump-topspin attempted | Jump-topspin live in rotation; ace-rate climbing; missed-serve discipline | Jump-topspin point-scoring weapon; serves to scout; in-match toss-adjust |
| **Defense** | Reactive; chases ball; out of position on tips | Reads hitter sometimes; pursuit OK on routine | Reads hitter approach + arm; libero-in-6 default [[defense-middle-back]]; pursuit chains 2 contacts | Reads setter eyes; calls coverage adjustments; emergency dig recovery | Reads setter + hitter; OOS dig-to-pass consistent; libero overhead second-ball |
| **Transition** | Block-to-approach broken; OOS bails fail | Transition swing on clean digs only; setter second-ball reactive | Block-to-approach in 3 steps; bail-to-pin reliable; OOS-to-attack live | First-ball-kill in transition; setter second-ball protocol; pipe transition | OOS-attack 30%+ kill rate; in-rotation transition adjust; libero overhead set |
| **Mental & coachability** | Inconsistent effort; defensive on feedback | Solid effort; takes feedback; emotional on errors | Compete-in-practice [aoc-2026-rose-no-free-balls]; pre-performance routine emerging | 8-week self-talk block completed; pressure-routine reliable; teammate accountability | Recruiting-grade competitor; full mental-skills toolkit; leadership behaviors |

## Scoring

Each evaluator scores all 8 rows independently per athlete. Sum the row scores for an aggregate (max 40). Apply the team-decision filter:

- **Roster-bid 17s-ready:** column 5 in 4+ rows AND no row below column 3. Flag for 17U placement consideration; if 16U is the only roster, she anchors it.
- **Roster-bid 16s standard:** column 3 across all 8 rows OR column 4 in 4+ rows with column 2 minimum elsewhere. This is the promotion bar from the [[16s]] age-guide and the default 16U roster offer.
- **Development bid:** column 2 in 2-3 rows with column 3 elsewhere. Roster-edge case — discuss in evaluator panel; consider position-specific development plan.
- **No bid:** column 1 in 2+ rows OR aggregate <18. Recommend 15U or club-development-team placement; communicate to family per [[club-ops-parent-comms-templates]].

Setter row applies only to athletes auditioning for setter; non-setters score on the other 7 rows (max 35 — adjust thresholds proportionally).

## Calibration notes

**Session structure (90 min target).** 10 min warm-up observation (athleticism + work ethic baseline). 20 min skill stations (passing wave + setting wave + hitting lines + serve/receive). 25 min position-specific (setter audit, libero pursuit, MB block-and-attack, OH back-row). 30 min controlled 6v6 with scoring rules that punish casual play [aoc-2026-rose-no-free-balls] (giveaway free balls = -1, missed serves = -2, back-row kills count double). 5 min cooldown + flex/jump-touch measure.

**Inter-rater agreement.** Three evaluators minimum; each scores independently before any panel discussion. After session, compare rows where evaluators disagree by 2+ points and re-watch the relevant clip if recorded. The bunch-read [aoc-2026-bunch-read-blocking] and libero-split [aoc-2024-sr-formations] reads are evaluator-skill-dependent — flag any evaluator whose blocking/passing scores diverge systematically from the panel mean for calibration video work pre-tryout.

**Common biases to avoid.** Recency-bias on the last drill of the session (rotate evaluation order). Approach-touch halo (a 9'8" touch is a recruiting datum, not a skill column). Familiarity-bias toward returning athletes vs. tryout-only candidates (score the rep observed, not the player history). Single-rep panic (column 3 means "consistent at age-appropriate level" — one mishit doesn't drop a column). Pressure-acclimatization gap [mesagno-2017-choking-models-interventions] — first-time tryout athletes may underperform their true level; offer a shorter return-block on day 2 if scheduling allows.

**Recruiting hand-off.** Column 5 athletes are recruiting-relevant; flag for [[recruiting]] timeline check (NCAA D1 contact opens June 15 after sophomore year). Approach-touch, standing reach, and jump-touch numbers logged pre-tryout-day with handheld jump-touch device — these are recruiting-card data and belong in the athlete file regardless of bid.

**Self-talk and pressure routine [walter-2019-self-talk-junior-athletes].** The mental-row column 4 references an 8-week self-talk block; ask returning athletes whether they've completed one (program-confirmable) — score honestly, don't penalize first-year athletes for not having had the install yet.
