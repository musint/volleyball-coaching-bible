# Volleyballmag — All seven PVF rosters, info, outlooks (Jan 22, 2024)

## Summary
Volleyballmag's team-by-team capsule preview of the PVF inaugural season, published two days before the league's first match. Confirms each franchise's head coach, key roster names, and venue. The single most useful per-team snapshot for the inaugural season — Volleyballmag (Tier 2) is the long-running U.S. volleyball-news outlet that tracked all three new pro leagues from announcement through launch.

## Key claims / ideas
- Confirms inaugural head coaches: **Todd Dagenais (Atlanta), Angel Perez (Columbus), Cathy George (Grand Rapids), Shelton Collier (Omaha), Amy Pauly (Orlando), Tayyiba Haneef-Park (San Diego), Fran Flory (Vegas)**
- **Six of seven head coaches are women.** Tayyiba Haneef-Park is a former U.S. Olympic indoor player; Fran Flory is the longtime former LSU head coach; Cathy George is the longtime former Michigan State head coach.
- Cecile Reynaud is league-office VP of Volleyball Operations, not a single-team head coach.
- Inaugural-match attendance: **11,624** at Atlanta vs. Omaha (Jan 24, 2024) — record for a U.S. women's pro volleyball match.
- **35 PVF players had been part of their respective country's Olympic or national-team systems** at launch; **17 had spent time in the USA national-team gym**.
