## Summary

Penn State 6v6 rotational side-out drill (2014 Toronto Clinic). Comparable middles/OH/opposites are paired across the net. Rose rotates through all six rotations by swapping setters, then LS+MB. Both sides alternate serves until one wins 3 rallies and banks a point. Scramble + repeat until all 6 rotations completed. Players report totals to the coach at the end.

## Key claims / ideas

- Pairing equal-skill players across the net prevents starters from beating up on non-starters.
- "Win 3 rallies" per rotation is the atomic unit of scoring.
- Rotation-through-all-six ensures each rotation is trained each drill.
- Player-reported totals build individual accountability without dictating playing time.
