# Chase to swing drill with Kevin Hambly

## Summary
Kevin Hambly's (Illinois) scramble drill: two back-row players must chase a tossed ball, dig, and produce a good enough second touch to give a waiting attacker a swing. Target is 5 good swings before the next pair rotates in.

## Key claims / ideas
- Out-of-system ball control is trainable — don't just let scramble reps happen by accident.
- Goal-threshold (5 good swings) scales intensity without time pressure.
- Doubles as a drill for outside hitters to practice swinging on sets from non-setters.
- Two-player chase-and-set forces real-time communication and role-switching.

## Topics covered
- Scramble defense to attack
- Out-of-system setting by non-setters
- Chase-and-dig training
