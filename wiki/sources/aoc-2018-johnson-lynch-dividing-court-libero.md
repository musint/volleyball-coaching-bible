# Dividing up the court on serve receive

## Summary
Christy Johnson-Lynch's libero-centric receive principle: when you have a strong libero, bias the receive pattern to give her more court, more responsibility, and more touches.

## Key claims / ideas
- A good libero should be getting as many touches as possible.
- Divide the court so the libero owns the largest area.
- Funneling passes to the best passer is the primary lever of serve-receive design.
- Receive-pattern construction follows personnel, not a fixed template.

## Topics covered
- Libero-weighted serve receive
- Court-division by passer skill
- Touch maximization for best passer
