# John Dunning – What kind of hitter are you?

## Summary
Dunning argues every hitter should know her own hitting style so she can invest in the strengths of that style and target the attacking gaps in it — a question he posed to every Stanford hitter.

## Key claims / ideas
- Hitters should be able to articulate what type of hitter they are.
- Self-classification lets players double down on strengths and identify what to improve.
- Different types of hitters require different training emphases.

## Topics covered
- Hitter identity / self-awareness
- Hitting style classification
- Player-directed development
- Attacking for outside and opposite hitters
