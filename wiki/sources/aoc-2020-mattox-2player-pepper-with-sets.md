# 2-player pepper with sets

## Summary
Mattox's 2-player pepper with hand sets. Count hand sets above the antenna before the ball hits the floor to gamify and challenge ball control across hitting, digging, and setting.

## Key claims / ideas
- Adding hand sets to pepper forces ball-control skill across all three skills.
- Track reps above the antenna as the scoring goal.
