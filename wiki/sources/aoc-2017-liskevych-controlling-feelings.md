# Terry Liskevych on controlling feelings and attitude

## Summary
A 2016 Atlanta Clinic lecture on the mental game: controlling emotions and converting them into a positive attitude that contributes to the team.

## Key claims / ideas
- Players and coaches must own their emotional state rather than being owned by it.
- Attitude is a choice that feeds team performance; negative emotion untreated becomes team drag.
- The job of the coach is to help players convert feelings into usable attitude.
- The mental game is trainable like technique.

## Topics covered
- Emotional control for athletes and coaches
- Attitude as a team contribution
- Sport psychology in coaching
- Mental game training
