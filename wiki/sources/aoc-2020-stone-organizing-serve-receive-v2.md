# Organizing serve receive

## Summary
Companion Jim Stone piece (2 days earlier than the /serve-receive-patterns/ version) restating the same two-lever rule: best passers cover max area, best hitters get freed up. Same OH-strong vs. RS-strong pattern shifts.

## Key claims / ideas
- Best passers should cover the most area.
- Best hitters should be freed from coverage to attack.
- Strong OH: shift receive right so the OH takes minimal coverage.
- Strong RS: OH takes more receive, RS gets a sliver and stays available to hit.

## Topics covered
- Serve-receive pattern design
- Personnel-driven reception shifts
