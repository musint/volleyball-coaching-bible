# Passing drill progression: Square, tilt, and weight transfer

## Summary
Ray Bechard demos a three-component passing progression — stay square to the ball, tilt the platform toward the target, transfer weight through the pass — starting from a simple toss drill and layering in a seated drill for isolated weight-transfer feel.

## Key claims / ideas
- Three fundamentals of effective passing: square, tilt, weight transfer
- Start with simple toss drill to drill platform angles and body alignment
- Minimize unnecessary motion — platform stays low and stable
- Seated drill isolates weight transfer and arm tilt (removes footwork as a variable)
- Good warmup / technical development progression

## Topics covered
- Passing fundamentals (square, tilt, weight transfer)
- Platform angle training
- Seated passing progression
- Minimizing unnecessary motion
