## Summary

Shauna Denney (president of 403 Selects Volleyball, Calgary) gives 10 pragmatic steps for launching a juniors club: diverse working board, quality coaching staff, niche identification, budget, code-of-conduct, targeted athlete recruiting, flexibility, clear communication, positive parent engagement, mandatory parent meeting + movie night (Bruce Brown "Role of Parents" video).

## Key claims / ideas

- Board must have coaches, teachers, business people, lawyers + experts in marketing/web/accounting — doers over talkers.
- Quality coaches are foundational; budget for their continued development (e.g., AOC membership).
- Every club needs a "niche" — differentiation from competing clubs.
- Enforced code of conduct signed by coaches, parents, athletes.
- Liskevych: failures are "opportunities to begin again more intelligently."
- $250 volunteer bond mechanism to convert parent labor into club capacity.
- Dr. Bruce Brown's "Role of Parents" video is the recommended parent-education artifact.
