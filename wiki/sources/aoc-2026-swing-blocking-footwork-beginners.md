# Swing blocking footwork for beginners

## Summary
Mark Rosen (Michigan) offers a beginner-friendly swing-blocking footwork progression — start at the 10-foot line, learn the steps without a jump, then add the jump while penetrating hands over the net.

## Key claims / ideas
- Players line up at the 10-foot line in middle blocker position.
- Teach the footwork first without a jump; only add the jump once steps are comfortable.
- Take aggressive, long steps.
- Penetrate the hands over the net on the block.
- Requires one player and a half court — minimal setup makes it usable as a warm-up.

## Topics covered
- Swing-block footwork progression
- Teaching sequence (footwork → jump)
- Hand penetration at the net
- Aggressive step length as a coaching cue
