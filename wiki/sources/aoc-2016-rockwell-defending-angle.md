## Summary

5-on-5 drill teaching aggressive 1-on-1 blocking and digging. Hitters must swing (no tips); blockers must hold the outside angle without cheating to center. Play to 5, winner stays.

## Key claims / ideas

- Head-up hitter-blocker matchup: the primary hitter option is cross-court, so the blocker's job is holding the outside without cheating center (which opens the line).
- No-tips rule forces block-and-dig decisions under hitter pressure, not soft contact.

## Topics covered

- 1-on-1 blocking positioning
- Defending cross-court vs. line
- Competitive 5-on-5 drill format
