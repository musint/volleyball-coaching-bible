# Chen Zhonghe — Wikipedia biography

## Summary

The Wikipedia biography of Chen Zhonghe (陈忠和) documents the head coach who anchored the China Women's National Team between the Yuan-Weimin 1980s era and the Lang-Ping 2010s era [chen-zhonghe-wikipedia-bio]. Chen served as China women's NT head coach from 2001 to 2009 and delivered the program's middle Olympic gold — Athens 2004 — together with the 2003 FIVB Women's World Cup gold [chen-zhonghe-wikipedia-bio]. The page describes Chen as "the man behind the gold medals of the 2003 FIVB Volleyball Women's World Cup and the 2004 Athens Olympics" [chen-zhonghe-wikipedia-bio]. The 2004 Athens gold is widely remembered for China's comeback from 0–2 down against Russia in the final. Chen received the 2003 Touching China Annual Person of the Year Award [chen-zhonghe-wikipedia-bio]. He was depicted in the 2020 film *Leap*, with actors Huang Bo and Peng Yuchang portraying aspects of his role; Chen reportedly objected to the depiction and threatened defamation action [chen-zhonghe-wikipedia-bio].
