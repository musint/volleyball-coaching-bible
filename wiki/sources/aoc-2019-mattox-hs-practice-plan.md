## Summary

Tod Mattox (Bishop's School) walks through a specific 40-minute HS practice plan: short-court and pepper to open, focused skill drills in the middle, and intense hitting drills to close.

## Key claims / ideas

- Practice has an ebb and flow that coaches can design for, not just observe.
- A HS practice can be productive in 40 minutes if the sequence is well-chosen.
- Opening with short-court/pepper warms technique; closing with hitting builds intensity where it matters most.

## Topics covered

40-minute HS practice template; practice sequencing (warmup → focused drill → intense hitting); team chemistry through flow.
