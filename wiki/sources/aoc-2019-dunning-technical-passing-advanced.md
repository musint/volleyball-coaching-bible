# Technical passing for advanced players

## Summary
John Dunning's five-point checklist for advanced passing: read before the serve leaves, move feet to line and depth, form platform early, calculate angle to target, and trust the contact.

## Key claims / ideas
- Know where the serve is going before it is served.
- Move feet to the line and depth of the serve.
- Form the platform before the feet arrive at destination.
- Calculate the best angle to direct the ball to target.
- Belief enables a simple, calm contact.

## Topics covered
- Advanced passing technique
- Pre-contact reading
- Platform-before-feet sequencing
- Mental framing of the contact
