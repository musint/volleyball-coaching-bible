# Middle transition drill: Hitting the first ball

## Summary
John Dunning's 6v6 progression for middle transition hitting. Start easy (middle can pull before the free ball comes), then tighten timing (free ball before pull complete), add slide off transition, then finish with block-outside to "1" or "c" (back-1) transition.

## Key claims / ideas
- Middle transition hitting must be trained under progressively tighter timing.
- Early reps: allow middle to begin transition before coach throws the free ball.
- Progression: free ball enters before pull completes, forcing faster transition.
- Add slide: requires full routing from transition through approach.
- Final phase: block outside (opponent RS) then transition to "1" or "c" (back-1).

- Middle transition progression
- Slide off transition
- Block-outside to quick-attack transition
