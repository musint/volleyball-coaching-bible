# Ideas and drills for training game-changing Liberos

## Summary
Christy Johnson-Lynch (Iowa State) and Kirsten Bernthal Booth argue a great libero is as critical as a great setter. They outline the libero profile and demo drills across ball control, passing, defense, and setting.

## Key claims / ideas
- A great libero is as critical to success as a great setter
- Libero profile: platform, ball handling, reaction time, consistency, second-ball setting, serve toughness, leadership, resilience
- Libero positioning depends on serve-receive formation and reads
- Drill categories for libero training: ball control, passing, defensive (digging), setting
- Second-ball setting is part of the libero skill set

## Topics covered
- Libero profile / characteristics
- Libero positioning in SR
- Ball control, passing, defense, setting drills for liberos
- Second-ball responsibilities
