# Hirofumi Daimatsu — Wikipedia

## Summary
Wikipedia biographical article on Hirofumi (Hirobumi) Daimatsu. Used in this
wiki to pin two facts the Hall of Fame record leaves underspecified: the **1953**
hire year at Nichibo Kaizuka, and the **1968** Liberal Democratic Party election
to the House of Councillors (the upper chamber of Japan's National Diet).
Corroborates the February 12, 1921 birth date, the November 24, 1978 death
date (age 57, Tokyo), and the Kansai Gakuin University playing background.
Does not discuss NHK television ratings, China coaching trip, or the
kaiten reshibu drill in any biographical detail.

## Key claims / ideas
- Hire year 1953: "In 1953 he became the coach for the factory women's volleyball team of Nichibo Kaizuka."
- 1968 election to House of Councillors on Liberal Democratic Party ticket.
- Born February 12, 1921, Ayauta-gun, Kagawa Prefecture.
- Died November 24, 1978, Tokyo, age 57.
- WWII service in Imphal operation (Burma / China theatre).
