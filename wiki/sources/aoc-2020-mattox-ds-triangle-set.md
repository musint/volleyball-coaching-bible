# 2-player + DS triangle set drill

## Summary
Mattox's triangle drill highlights the footwork difference between passing and setting: one player passes, two set with hands. Shuttle progression rotates players through positions after each touch.

## Key claims / ideas
- Passing and setting use different footwork; a mixed drill makes the difference visible.
- Triangle: 1 passer + 2 setters = all three skills in one rep.
- Shuttle progression (adding a 4th player) rotates every player through every role.

## Topics covered
- Passing vs. setting footwork contrast
- Triangle shuttle drill
- Cross-skill development
