# Butterfly drill

## Summary
Tod Mattox: the Butterfly drill is a staple — players serve from half court, progressing to behind the service line. Works serving, passing, and reinforces squaring up on sets.

## Key claims / ideas
- Butterfly is a gym staple — most volleyball programs run some variant
- Start serving from half court, progress to behind the service line
- Hones passing along with serving
- Reinforces squaring to the ball while setting
- Three-skill drill in one flow

## Topics covered
- Butterfly drill mechanics
- Multi-skill chaining (serve → pass → set)
- Squaring up on sets
- Progression from half-court to full service
