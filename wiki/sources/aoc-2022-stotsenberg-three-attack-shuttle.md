# Setting: Three attack shuttle drill

## Summary
Stotsenberg's shuttle drill reps three setter dumps in sequence: left-hand net-line dump to Zone 2/3, dump to the donut, right-hand backward dump to Zone 4. Each setter rotates through all three before the next takes a turn.

## Key claims / ideas
- Setters should develop three dumps they can execute on command.
- Rep 1: left-hand dump near net, Zone 2 or 3.
- Rep 2: dump to the donut (mid-court).
- Rep 3: right-hand backward dump to Zone 4.
