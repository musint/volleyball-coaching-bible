# Team receive drill

## Summary
Tod Mattox's team receive drill reinforces rotation positioning: team passes serves from two opposing servers, must be in correct SR position for each of the six rotations. Progresses from setter-catch to live set + attack.

## Key claims / ideas
- Primary focus: making sure every player is in the correct SR position for each of the six rotations
- Two servers deliver serves from the other side
- Start simple: setter catches each pass (trains position, not offense)
- Progress: setter sets and team attacks (adds transition and offense)
- Drill reinforces rotation learning

## Topics covered
- Rotation-specific SR positioning
- Six-rotation team drill
- Setter-catch progression for beginners
