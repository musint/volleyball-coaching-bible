## Summary

Holly McPeak on bump-setting a ball falling fast to the sand: get the body as low as possible to buy contact time and deliver an up-and-down set. Demoed by Katrina Kernochan (USC-bound).

## Key claims / ideas

- On scramble/broken plays, going low maximizes contact time — the decisive variable for set quality.
- Indoor principle transfer: same mechanic applies when setter is pulled way off-net.
