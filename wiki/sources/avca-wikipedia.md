# American Volleyball Coaches Association — Wikipedia overview

## Summary
The Wikipedia entry on the AVCA consolidates the association's awards history — Women's Division I National Coach of the Year (1982–present), other divisional coach-of-the-year awards, Hall of Fame coach roster, and awards-by-school table. For this wiki it serves as a secondary ingest anchor for AVCA-award claims on individual coach pages where AVCA's own paginated awards-directory is not easily queried.

## Key claims / ideas
- **Women's Division I National Coach of the Year — Mary Wise (Florida)**: 1992, 1996, 2017 (three wins). Multi-winners through 2025 also include Russ Rose (5), John Cook (3), John Dunning (2), Terry Pettit (2), Dave Shoji (2), Heather Olmstead (1) / Shawn Olmstead (1).
- AVCA Hall of Fame coach roster captured verbatim (induction years not listed on page). Notable volleyball-coaching-bible-relevant inductees include Banachowski, Beal, Cook, Dunning, Dunphy, Hebert, Haley, Kiraly, Liskevych, McGown, Pettit, Rose, Shoji, Stone — providing a cross-check for the "in-the-AVCA-HoF" status referenced on multiple coach profiles in this wiki.
- Mary Wise is **not** on the AVCA Hall of Fame coaches roster as of the 2026-04-24 fetch; her AVCA recognition of record is "All-Time Great Coach" (2006), documented on her own Wikipedia biography but distinct from the formal HoF.
