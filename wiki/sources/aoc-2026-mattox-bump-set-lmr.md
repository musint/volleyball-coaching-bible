# Improve bump setting with left, middle, right drill

## Summary
Tod Mattox teaches bump setting (forearm-deliver-to-hitter) differently from regular passing: lift with the legs, angle the platform, minimize arm swing. Partner drill: 30 ft apart, cycle left-middle-right origin points to rep each angle.

## Key claims / ideas
- Bump setting is legwork-driven, unlike passing.
- Mechanics: legs lift, platform angled, minimal arm swing.
- Left-middle-right sequence trains all three delivery angles.

## Topics covered
- Bump setting mechanics
- Platform angling
- Drill: left-middle-right pair
