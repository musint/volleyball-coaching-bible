# 9 reasons teams fail to reach their potential

## Summary
Championship Performance applies organizational-leadership failure modes to sports teams: a nine-item checklist spanning unclear purpose, faulty strategy, weak accountability, poor team culture, and absent consequences.

## Key claims / ideas
- Unclear direction/purpose and faulty strategy are upstream causes of underperformance.
- Roles not linked to strategy and unclear accountability corrode execution.
- Negative team culture and compatibility issues between members degrade output.
- Lack of performance measures and lack of consequences for detrimental actions enable repeated failure.
- The same organizational failure modes apply equally to business teams and athletic teams.
