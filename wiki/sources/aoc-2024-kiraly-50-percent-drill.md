# Karch Kiraly: 50 percent drill

## Summary
Kiraly's "50 percent" 6v6 drill: every player rotates through every position (new setter every rotation) and attacks cannot exceed half-speed, forcing creative shot-making, long rallies, and many touches. Scoring 12-all to 15.

## Key claims / ideas
- Attack cap at 50 percent speed forces tips, rolls, off-speed shots, and placement over power.
- Rotation through every position exposes players to full-position awareness and a new setter each rotation.
- Rallies run long; kill hunting is hard — prioritizes ball control and creative scoring.
- Compressed scoring window (12-all → 15) keeps intensity up.

- Off-speed / tip / roll shot development
- Multi-position rotation drills
- Short-game compressed scoring
