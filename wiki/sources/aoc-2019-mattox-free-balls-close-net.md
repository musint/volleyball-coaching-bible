# Dealing with free balls that are close to the net

## Summary
Bishop's School players practice the decision tree on close-to-net free balls: full swing for a kill, two-hand redirect over, or high set back to teammates.

## Key claims / ideas
- Close-to-net free balls present three options: attack, redirect, or set back.
- Decision hinges on where the ball arrives and the player's read.
- Confidence on first ball comes from repeated decision reps.

- First-ball decision making.
