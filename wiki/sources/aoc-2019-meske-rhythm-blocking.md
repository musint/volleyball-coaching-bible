# Rhythm blocking drill

## Summary
Louisville's Dan Meske runs a four-rep rhythm blocking drill (left-middle-left-middle) where a single blocker jumps, lands on both feet, and turns back to center rapidly against two coaches hitting from the opposite side.

## Key claims / ideas
- Four consecutive blocks in rapid sequence: left, middle, left, middle.
- Two coaches on opposite side (middle front and right front) feed balls.
- Land on two feet; turn back to middle quickly each rep.
- Trains block-movement control under load.

## Topics covered
- Blocking footwork and landing.
- Movement density / conditioning.
- Turn-and-go after landing.
