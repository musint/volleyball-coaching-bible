# Unstoppable France Secure Second Straight Olympic Gold in Paris — Volleyball World, 10 August 2024

## Summary

FIVB / Volleyball World match-day coverage of France's 3-0 win over Poland in the men's volleyball gold-medal match at Paris 2024 (25-19, 25-20, 25-23 on 10 August 2024). Carries Andrea Giani's most-cited public quote — his "35 years" reflection on finally winning Olympic gold after collecting three medals as an Italian player without ever capturing the top step.

## Key claims / ideas

- Giani's post-final quote: "I'm very happy, it was my dream. I've been waiting for this medal for 35 years." The "35 years" frames his continuous senior international career arc from his 1988 Italy debut under [[julio-velasco]] through his 2024 Paris gold as France's coach.
- Final result: France 3-0 Poland, 25-19 / 25-20 / 25-23.
- France's path to the final: 3-2 reverse-sweep over Germany (QF); 3-0 over reigning World Champion Italy (SF); 3-0 over Poland (final).
- Top scorers in the final: Jean Patry (Opp) 17 points; Trévor Clévenot (OH) 11 points; Earvin N'Gapeth (OH) 8 points; Quentin Jouffroy (MB, off the bench) 4 aces.
- France became the third nation to defend men's Olympic gold and the first to do so in 36 years.
