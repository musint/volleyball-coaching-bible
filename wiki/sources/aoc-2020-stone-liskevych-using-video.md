# Using Video to Boost Your Performance

## Summary

A session from AOC's 2020 Virtual Clinic series, produced during the COVID-19 shutdown, with Jim Stone (U.S. Girls Youth National Team head coach; former Ohio State head coach) and Terry Liskevych (former U.S. Women's National Team head coach). The session organizes video use into three buckets: **scouting**, **match analysis**, and **teaching skills and tactics** — both online (relevant during the shutdown) and embedded in live practice. Live Q&A from watching coaches followed.

Liskevych also contributed three AOC-hosted downloadable resources that extend the session: a **Highlight Videos** guide, a **Use of Video in a Practice Setting** guide, and a **Scouting 101** document. These are the pieces that make this session function as more than a talking-heads clip — they operationalize the three buckets.

## Key claims / ideas

- Three buckets of video use in volleyball coaching: scouting, match analysis, teaching.
- Teaching applications split further into online / remote (especially relevant in spring 2020) and in-practice embedded review.
- Liskevych's three companion handouts: Highlight Videos, Use of Video in a Practice Setting, Scouting 101.
- Session is freely accessible (not paywalled) — AOC open-clinic content.

## Topics covered

- Video scouting workflow
- Match video analysis
- Practice-integrated video teaching
- Remote / online video-based coaching (COVID-era use case)
