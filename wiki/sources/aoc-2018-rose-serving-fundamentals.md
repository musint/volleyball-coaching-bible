# Serving fundamentals with Russ Rose

## Summary
Short drill clip: players line up and serve one at a time, with coach-led debrief after each serve on technique, placement, hand-to-ball contact, and outcome quality.

## Key claims / ideas
- A strong serve is key to any team's success.
- Single-server-at-a-time format allows focused coaching feedback.
- Feedback should cover four dimensions: technique, placement, hand-to-ball contact, and whether the outcome was considered successful.

## Topics covered
- Serving technique fundamentals
- Feedback-heavy drill structure
- Defining serve "success" (outcome vs execution)
