# Bewitched: How seeds of Japan's 1964 gold in women's volleyball were sewn over a decade

## Summary
Official IOC editorial (Olympics.com) retrospective on the 1964 Tokyo Olympic
women's volleyball gold. This source's decisive contribution to the wiki is
the **NHK television-ratings figure for the final**: 66.8% average rating
with a peak above 80% during the match. Cites NHK directly and notes the
match remains the most-watched sporting event in Japanese television history
(statement as of the 2021 publication — 57 years after the event).

Corroborating coverage at theolympians.co (Roy Tomizawa, 2019) reports the
same 66.8% figure in a tabular comparison of 1964 Olympic broadcasts.

## Key claims / ideas
- NHK final rating: **66.8% average**, peak **>80%**.
- Most-watched sporting event in Japanese television history (as of 2021, 57 years on).
- Match played October 23, 1964, Komazawa Olympic Park, Tokyo.
- Japan defeated USSR 3-0 for inaugural women's Olympic volleyball gold.
