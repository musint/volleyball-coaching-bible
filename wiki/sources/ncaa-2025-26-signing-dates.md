# 2025-26 NCAA Signing Dates

## Summary

The NCAA's authoritative 2025-26 signing-date calendar, covering the written offer of athletics aid across all Division I and II sports. This document replaces the older National Letter of Intent (NLI) early/regular signing-period structure, which was absorbed into the NCAA written-offer framework in 2024-25. For women's volleyball — which falls under "All Other Division I and II Sports" — the 2025-26 initial signing date is November 12, 2025, with final signing dates set by each institution's scholarship-awarding policy. There is no separate April "regular" period for volleyball under the current framework; the April 15, 2026 regular signing date is specific to D1 basketball.

## Key claims / ideas

- 2025-26 initial signing date for "All Other Division I and II Sports" (including women's volleyball): **November 12, 2025**.
- Final signing date for the same category: based on institutional policy for awarding scholarships.
- D1 basketball-only dates: early period November 12-19, 2025; regular period opening April 15, 2026.
- D1 football: early period December 3-5, 2025; regular period opening February 4, 2026.
- NCAA signing dates do not apply to four-year transfers; those sign via the NCAA Transfer Portal within the sport-specific transfer window.
- The pre-2024 NLI "early November (women's volleyball) and April (regular)" framing common in older recruiting guides has been superseded; volleyball now has a single mid-November initial signing date with institutional finalization.
