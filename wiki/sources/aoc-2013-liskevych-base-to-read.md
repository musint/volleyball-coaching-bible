## Summary

Terry Liskevych (3x USA Olympic coach) drill covering the base-position to read-position transition in team defense. Filmed at 2013 Dayton Clinic.

## Key claims / ideas

- Team defense starts from a base position and transitions to read position as the opponent's attack develops.
- Base-to-read is a trainable transition habit, not an instinct.
- Reading begins at base, not after the attacker commits.

## Topics covered

Base-to-read defensive transition; reading habits; team-defense starting positions.
