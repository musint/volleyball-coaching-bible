# Plus 45 transition drill with Lizzy Stemke

## Summary
Lizzy Stemke's (Georgia) 3v6 drill forces 45 structured transition attacks: 15 balls from each entry zone (outside / middle / opposite), subdivided into 5-ball blocks with pre-assigned set targets. Every transition attack is pre-scripted to a middle, opposite, or outside set.

## Key claims / ideas
- 45-ball structure with fixed 5-ball micro-blocks keeps volume and script tight.
- 3 entry zones (outside, middle, opposite) × 3 set targets (middle, opposite, outside) cycles the transition offense through every combination.
- 3v6 format puts the team of 6 in the transition-and-attack role; blockers on the other side give a live read.
- Forces focus and fundamental execution at high volume.

## Topics covered
- Structured transition volume drill
- Entry-zone cycling
- Pre-scripted set targets in transition
