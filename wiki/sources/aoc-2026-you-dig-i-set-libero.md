# You dig, I set drill for liberos

## Summary
Two-libero drill: dig-then-set with deceptive set choices on the pins. Difficulty progresses with harder hits, further tosses, or deceptive delivery so the second libero must read who takes the first ball.

## Key claims / ideas
- Two liberos in back row, catchers at the pins.
- First libero passes; second libero becomes the setter to a pin.
- Coaches push deception: vary the set choice, hit harder, toss further away.
- Trains libero-as-setter decision making under defensive pressure.

## Topics covered
- Libero bump-setting to pins.
- Dig-to-set partner drill.
- Deception in set selection.
