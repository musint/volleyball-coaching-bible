# Men's Volleyball at Tokyo 2020 (Wikipedia)

## Summary

Wikipedia reference for the men's volleyball tournament at the Tokyo 2020 Olympic Games (held in 2021), with full bracket and results. Captures the France gold-medal run under head coach **Laurent Tillie** — a 4-seed-out-of-pool finish that turned into a quarterfinal-through-final knockout sweep, capped by a 3-2 final over the Russian Olympic Committee with **Earvin N'Gapeth** named tournament MVP.

## Key claims / ideas

- France finished Pool B at 2-3 (loss to USA, wins over Tunisia and Argentina), entering knockouts as a 4-seed.
- **Quarterfinal (Aug 3, 2021):** France def. Poland 3-2 (25-21, 22-25, 25-21, 21-25, 15-9).
- **Semifinal (Aug 5, 2021):** France def. Argentina 3-0 (25-22, 25-19, 25-22).
- **Gold-medal match (Aug 7, 2021):** **France def. Russian Olympic Committee 3-2 (25-23, 25-17, 21-25, 21-25, 15-12).**
- France's first Olympic men's volleyball gold.
- Tournament MVP: **Earvin N'Gapeth (France)**.
- Bronze: Argentina (def. Brazil 3-2).
