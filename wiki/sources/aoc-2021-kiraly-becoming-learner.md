# Karch Kiraly on becoming a learner

## Summary
Video-backed P/ATH Sports lesson (host attribution on AOC is "P/ATH Sports"; featuring Karch Kiraly) with a structured takeaway/reflection/practice format. The message: discomfort is normal when learning; mistakes are opportunities, but only if you treat them as such with patience and self-talk awareness.

## Key claims / ideas
- Feeling uncomfortable while learning something new is normal and necessary, not a warning sign.
- Elite performers still struggle while learning — they've learned to embrace the discomfort.
- Common failure mode: negative self-talk, shrinking, and embarrassment after mistakes.
- Mistakes only become improvement opportunities if paired with patience and a deliberate response.
- Practice tools: tell a teammate one in-sport and one out-of-sport thing you'll work on, then check in; catch your own post-mistake reactions in the moment.

## Topics covered
- Learner mindset and embracing discomfort
- Mistake response and self-talk
- Mental skills for players
- Transferable life lessons from sport
- Peer accountability practices
