# Fundamental volleyball drill: Triangle passing

## Summary
Joe Sagula's triangle passing drill — three players, passer / target-at-net / tosser-across-net — drills platform angle and post-contact hold. A foot-stagger variation teaches taking the ball outside the body for tough float serves.

## Key claims / ideas
- Triangle passing: passer + target at net + tosser across net
- Emphasis on good platform angle and holding the platform after contact (quiet platform)
- Real-time coach feedback without interrupting the drill
- Variation: one foot slightly forward to take the ball outside the body on a horizontal plane
- That variation is the key technique for handling tough float serves

## Topics covered
- Triangle passing drill
- Platform angles and holding
- Passing float serves
- Lateral body-plane pass technique
