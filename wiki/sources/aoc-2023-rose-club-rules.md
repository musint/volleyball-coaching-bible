# Russ Rose's rules for club season

## Summary
Russ Rose shares three rules for club coaches at the start of the club season, emphasizing patience with team form, investment in team chemistry, and a non-negotiable standard of effort.

## Key claims / ideas
- Don't over-emphasize reaching mid-season form at the beginning of club season.
- Use early season to build interpersonal skills and chemistry among players who may not be used to each other.
- Players must ALWAYS play hard, regardless of season phase or system experimentation.

## Topics covered
- Early-club-season priorities
- Team chemistry and interpersonal development
- Effort as a constant standard
