# Adjusting sets for middles with different tempos

## Summary
In a 5-1 with two middles operating at different tempos, Mattox advocates adjusting setting speed to match the superior hitter rather than splitting the difference. Play toward strength.

## Key claims / ideas
- In a 5-1 with two middles of differing tempo capability, adjust setting speed to the superior hitter.
- Splitting the difference produces the worst of both.

## Topics covered
- Tempo matching between setter and middles
- Handling tempo variance across middles in a 5-1
