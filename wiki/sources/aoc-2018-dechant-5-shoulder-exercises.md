## Summary

This is AOC curricular content; defer to qualified AT/CSCS/MD on programming specifics. Zach Dechant (Assistant Strength & Conditioning Coach, TCU) names five shoulder exercises for prehab and weakness correction: lateral band walk-out, waiter walks, "Y"s, wall slides, external rotation.

## Key claims / ideas

- Five-exercise shoulder prehab menu for volleyball players (from TCU S&C).
- Movements cover the major shoulder-stabilizer recruitment patterns: scap band, isometric load under anti-flexion (waiter walk), lower-trap "Y", scap upward rotation (wall slide), and rotator-cuff ER.
- Prehab is framed in explicit injury-prevention terms, not pure performance.

## Topics covered

- Shoulder prehab programming
- Rotator-cuff-specific work (external rotation)
- Scapular stabilizers ("Y", wall slide, band walk-out)
- Anti-flexion carry (waiter walk)
