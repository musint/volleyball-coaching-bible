# Creative ways to make passing competitive and fun

## Summary
Springfield College's Moira Long shows ways to make passing practice competitive: rolling chairs for platform angles, server-vs-passer scoring, and 0-3 pass rankings tracked on a whiteboard for visible progress.

## Key claims / ideas
- Rolling chairs help players isolate and train platform angles
- Server vs. passer competition rewards quality touches, not just reps
- 0-3 pass ranking — whiteboards surface progress over time
- Goal beyond technique: awareness, accountability, motivation
- Fun + competition + measurable goals = retention

## Topics covered
- Competitive passing drill design
- Platform angle training
- Pass-rating scoring systems
- Player motivation and accountability
