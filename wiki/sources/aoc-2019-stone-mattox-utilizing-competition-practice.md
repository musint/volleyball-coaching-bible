## Summary

Jim Stone and Tod Mattox synthesize the HS Prep Series with a culminating discussion of how to simulate match competition inside practice, offering a variety of competition styles, drills, and formats.

## Key claims / ideas

- Practice exists to prepare players for competition, so competition should be explicit in practice design.
- Multiple competition styles (individual, small-group, team-on-team) should appear in a season.
- Drill format choices affect what kind of competitive behavior players rehearse.

## Topics covered

Competition-in-practice taxonomy; match-simulation drills; competitive format selection.
