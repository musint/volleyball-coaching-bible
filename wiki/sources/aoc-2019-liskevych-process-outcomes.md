## Summary

Terry Liskevych (3x USA Olympic coach) argues that process precedes outcome and that accountability during the process is the non-negotiable prerequisite for positive outcomes. Includes the directive to "fall in love with the process."

## Key claims / ideas

- Winning matters, but process matters at least as much.
- No positive outcome is possible without accountability during the process.
- Process = practices + drills + scrimmages + skill practice + team bonding.
- Coach's job is to instill a love of day-to-day process in players, not only the euphoria of winning.

## Topics covered

Process-vs-outcome philosophy; accountability; daily-practice mindset; team-bonding as process element.
