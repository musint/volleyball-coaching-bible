# Dagenais Pursues Professional Volleyball Opportunity As Winningest UCF Volleyball Coach Over 15 Seasons

## Summary
April 27, 2023 UCF Athletics announcement covering Dagenais's resignation to pursue a professional head-coaching opportunity (later confirmed as PVF Atlanta). Provides program-building framing, final-tenure accolades (308-159; five consecutive AAC titles 2018-2022; six NCAA appearances; 62 academic honors; 3.68 combined GPA — highest of any UCF sport at time of departure), and on-record statements from Dagenais and AD Terry Mohajir.

## Key claims / ideas
- Resignation announced effective end of spring 2023; associate head coach Jenny Maurer became acting head coach.
- Final UCF record: 308-159 (.660); AAC record noted as 227 wins with 142 conference wins — AAC all-time winningest volleyball coach.
- Six NCAA Tournament appearances and five consecutive AAC titles (2018-2022) across the tenure.
- Every-semester 3.0+ team GPA since 2008; 62 conference academic honors.
- Dagenais: "My opportunity at UCF has given me the experience and confidence to go and help start a professional league."
- Mohajir: "Todd has done a fabulous job of building UCF volleyball into a national program."
