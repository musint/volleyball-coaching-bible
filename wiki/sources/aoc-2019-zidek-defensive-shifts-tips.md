## Summary

Marie Zidek (DePaul) diggers-vs-hitters drill. 3 diggers on knees in front of 10-foot line; 1 setter + 3 hitters from right/middle/left front. Digger team scores on any retrievable touch; hitting team scores on non-usable dig. Play to 5. Teaches diggers to shift based on setter tendency + hitter shoulder/elbow.

## Key claims / ideas

- Knees-down positioning forces diggers to rely on shift reads rather than reach.
- Shoulder and elbow of hitter are named cues.
- Setter tendency is explicit input to digger shift.
