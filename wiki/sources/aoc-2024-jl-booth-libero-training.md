# Ideas and drills for training game-changing Liberos

## Summary
Iowa State's Christy Johnson-Lynch and Creighton's Kirsten Bernthal Booth hold liberos at setter-equivalent importance and walk through libero characteristics, SR formations, reading, positioning, and a drill suite (ball control, passing, defense, setting).

## Key claims / ideas
- Great libero = as critical as a great setter.
- Libero characteristics: platform, ball handling, reaction time, consistency, second-ball setting, serve toughness, leadership, resilience.
- Coverage: serve-receive formations, reading, libero court positioning.
- Drill areas: ball control, passing, defense, setting.

## Topics covered
- Libero profile and selection criteria.
- Libero-centered SR formations.
- Reading and positioning.
- Libero drill library overview.
