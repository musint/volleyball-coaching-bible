# Nikola Grbić (Wikipedia)

## Summary

Wikipedia biographical article on Nikola Grbić (b. September 6, 1973, Klek, Zrenjanin, Serbia), legendary setter-turned-coach. Player career 1990s-2010 across Yugoslavia/Serbia + 20-year Italian Serie A run; **2000 Sydney Olympic gold (Yugoslavia), 1996 Atlanta bronze, 1998 Worlds silver**; national-team captain 1999-2010; 2016 Volleyball Hall of Fame induction. Coaching career: Sir Safety Perugia 2014, Serbia men's NT 2015-2017, **Poland men's NT January 12, 2022 - present**. Polish results under Grbić: 2022 Worlds silver, 2023 European Championship gold, 2023 + 2025 Nations League gold, 2024 Paris Olympic silver, 2025 Worlds bronze. Setter-perspective coaching of one of the most decorated modern programs.
