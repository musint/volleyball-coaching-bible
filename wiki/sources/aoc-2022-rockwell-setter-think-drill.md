## Summary

"Go Against the Flow" setter drill. Two pin hitters + two setters on the net, one middle blocker opposite. Setter must redirect the set to the pin the middle is *not* sneaking toward. Advance: have the middle sneak either direction.

## Key claims / ideas

- Setter IQ drills should couple physical redirection with real-time opposing-blocker reads.
- Training against a live moving blocker (even simplified) is higher fidelity than tossed reps.

## Topics covered

- Setter decision-making vs. blocker movement
- Setter/blocker cat-and-mouse
- Pin-to-pin setter redirection
