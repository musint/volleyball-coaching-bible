# Russ Rose: Analysis of Penn State match preparation

## Summary
Seminar from the 2017 Las Vegas Advanced Clinic in which Rose describes how to analyze matches — his own team's and opponents' — to maximize next-match performance and manage in-match coaching decisions.

## Key claims / ideas
- Worry about the game at hand, not a match three games ahead.
- Know the strengths and weaknesses of the opposing team.
- Track which rotations you're scoring in; engineer ways to stay in those rotations longer.
- Allow the team to bring each other's competitiveness out rather than forcing it from the bench.
- Coaching during matches has two distinct modes: things going perfectly vs. things going terribly — each requires different behavior.
- Rose took "incessant notes" on every Penn State match as the basis for his analysis habit.

## Topics covered
- Match analysis methodology
- Opponent scouting
- Rotation-by-rotation point tracking
- In-match coaching decisions
- Team-driven competitive culture
