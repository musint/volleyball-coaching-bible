# Wisconsin blocking progression for individual hand work

## Summary
Wisconsin's three-part progression trains blocking vision and independent-hand blocking using tennis balls: simultaneous catch, staggered catch, then one-handed block against a coach reading the shoulder.

## Key claims / ideas
- Drill 1: blocker catches a tennis ball in each hand simultaneously (trains both eyes/hands).
- Drill 2: tosser staggers throws so blocker reacts to each hand independently.
- Drill 3: against coach on a box, blocker reads the shoulder and blocks one-handed.
- Progression isolates hand independence and shoulder-reading without full live hitting.

## Topics covered
- Individual-hand blocking.
- Vision and shoulder reading.
- Tennis-ball-based hand-work drill.
