# Mary Wise — Leadership Profile

## Summary
A leadership-focused profile of Mary Wise, the longtime Florida head coach (1991–2024) who finished her career with 987–150 at Florida and 1,068 total victories — the most wins by any female D-I volleyball head coach. The piece frames Wise's coaching as relationship-centric: "The answers are always in relationships." Her philosophy statement, "help every player find and hone their superpower," signals a player-differentiation approach rather than a mold-fitting one.

Wise's "shared-ownership" mantra — "It's your team, what do you expect?" — pushes players into co-authorship of the program's standards. She pairs this with open-door access (players bring non-volleyball issues to her office) and a "family meeting" framing for team business. Colleagues describe her as simultaneously "competitive, fiery, passionate" and deeply caring, a combination that produced 25 SEC regular-season titles, 34 consecutive NCAA tournament appearances, and two national championship finals (2003, 2017).

## Key claims / ideas
- "The answers are always in relationships."
- "Help every player find and hone their superpower."
- Shared ownership: "It's your team, what do you expect?"
- Team meetings as "family meetings" — continuity beyond graduation.
- Competitive intensity + genuine care are not in tension.
- Measure success by impact on lives, not win totals.
