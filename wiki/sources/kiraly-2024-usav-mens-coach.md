# Huge move: Karch Kiraly will coach the USA men's national volleyball team

## Summary
Published October 24, 2024 on VolleyballMag.com. This is the news article confirming that Karch Kiraly, after 12 years coaching the USA Women's National Team, would transition to head coach of the USA men's national team for the LA 2028 cycle. The opening was created when John Speraw, the outgoing men's head coach, was appointed president and CEO of USA Volleyball the month prior.

The article is the single best cite for the 2024 coaching transition itself. It is paired in the raw file with the ESPN report (espn.com id 41983669) that adds Kiraly's personal transition quotes ("For one-quarter of my life, I've poured everything I have into the U.S. women's team…"). Together these establish the authoritative narrative of why he moved and what the USAV leadership said publicly about it.

## Key claims / ideas
- Kiraly transitions from USWNT head coach (2012-2024) to USMNT head coach for the 2028 Olympic cycle.
- The opening was created by John Speraw's move to USAV president and CEO.
- Speraw's framing, per the article: "It feels right that Karch Kiraly should be coaching the men in the Los Angeles Olympics in 2028."
- Speraw on Kiraly as coach: "He's the greatest of all time… he's going to be amazing."
- Kiraly's own transition quotes (from parallel ESPN coverage stored in the raw file): "For one-quarter of my life, I've poured everything I have into the U.S. women's team. Now, it's time for new adventures and new challenges." / "I am thrilled beyond words at the opportunity to coach the U.S. men's national team."
- Speraw's historical framing: "Our record of success in the sport began when Karch led Team USA to the gold medal in 1984."
