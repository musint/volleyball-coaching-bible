# Blocking drill: Reps off a serve and pass

## Summary
Jed Stotsenberg (Wave VB San Diego) designs a high-volume blocking drill: 3 blockers + servers on one side, SR team on other, blockers block-only (no transition swing). Maximizes blocking reps and reading reps with coach feedback.

## Key claims / ideas
- Three blockers plus servers on one side; SR team in LS/M/RS positions opposite
- Play begins with serve, runs like a point until attack
- Blockers block or touch only — no transition swing — keeps drill fast
- Coach entry balls when servers miss speed up the drill further
- Coaching points: hand placement (L/R side blockers), pin-blocker positioning, lining up against hitters

## Topics covered
- Blocking drill design in SR context
- Reading the hitter off a pass
- Pin-blocker vs. middle-blocker hand placement
- Removing transition to increase block reps
