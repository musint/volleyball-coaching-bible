# Competitive serving and receiving drill from Sweden

## Summary
Steve Colpus (Norco VB Club) runs a chaotic two-ball serve-and-receive drill: two teams, two simultaneous balls, strict 2-arm pass / set contact rules, and a "double-ball-down" scoring mechanic with erase-points for rapid recovery.

## Key claims / ideas
- Two teams of 6-8 players, 3-2-3 alignment for 8-player teams
- Both teams serve and receive simultaneously with two balls
- Legal contacts: two forearm pass or set only — no blocking/hitting/jumping/one-arm digs
- Scoring: if both balls fall on opposing team's side at once, opposing team gets a point unless they "erase" by getting a ball back into play before the second ball scores
- Missed serves can be retrieved and re-served before opponent scores
- Requirements: 12-16 players, ball cart, full court, coach

## Topics covered
- Two-ball simultaneous drill
- Competitive serve-receive chaos
- Erase-point mechanic
- Multi-skill training in one drill
