## Summary

Coach Connection LIVE session *The Role Revolution: Transforming Playing Time Decisions into Team Growth Opportunities* with mental performance coach Amanda Jones. Addresses one of the hardest coach-management challenges of a season: making playing time and role decisions while keeping the whole team motivated and engaged.

## Key claims / ideas

- Playing time decisions are a mental-performance problem, not only a tactical one.
- Parent who approaches coach immediately after a game about playing time needs a scripted response, not an improvised one.
- Pre-meeting steps with parents concerning playing time protect both sides.
- Hard conversations with athletes must account for mental health without becoming therapy.
- Negative parent comments must be processed, not internalized.
- Tryout questions can reveal "we" vs "me" orientation before roles are assigned.

## Topics covered

Playing time communication; parent management; player-role clarity; mental health in coach-athlete conversations; tryout evaluation for team-fit.
