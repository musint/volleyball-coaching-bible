# Down-ball and free-ball defense: Jim Stone chalk talk

## Summary
Jim Stone chalk-talk defining down ball vs. free ball, prescribing defensive shapes for each, explaining setter reactions in free-ball situations and blocker behavior on down balls, and emphasizing attacker-reading awareness on down balls.

## Key claims / ideas
- Down ball and free ball are distinct situations demanding distinct defensive shapes.
- Setter has specific free-ball responsibilities that differ from hard-driven responses.
- Blockers have different roles on a down ball than on a hard attack.
- Reading the attacker's body orientation predicts ball destination — awareness is the key defensive skill.

## Topics covered
- Down-ball vs. free-ball distinction
- Defensive shape per ball type
- Setter and blocker free-ball / down-ball roles
- Attacker-reading on down balls
