# Pairs toss drill

## Summary
Tod Mattox's Pairs Toss: partner tosses, passer freezes platform until partner catches. Switch after 5. Emphasis: low platform with short stroke, not high platform with long swing.

## Key claims / ideas
- One tosses, one passes; passer freezes platform until partner catches the pass
- After 5 passes, switch roles
- Technical emphasis: low platform, short stroke (not swinging up with a long stroke)
- Beginner / intermediate fundamentals drill

## Topics covered
- Frozen-platform training
- Short-stroke passing technique
- Beginner pairs drill
