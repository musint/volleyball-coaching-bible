# Team defense and reading

## Summary
Andy Halaz layers partner, three-person, and 6v6 drills to teach diggers to be stopped at contact, read behind the block, and shift between base, perimeter, and rotation defense — with specific 14U adjustments like wing defenders up at the 10-foot line.

## Key claims / ideas
- Diggers must be fully stopped before contact.
- Adding a blocker in drills trains reading deflected balls and hits around the block.
- At 14U, wing defenders start up at the 10-foot line in base because of frequent tips/soft hits.
- Wing blockers bunch to help middle; team shifts to perimeter or rotation defense by situation.
- On bad passes, middle and right-side blocker shift to block the left-side pin — the predictable set at this level when off-the-net.

## Topics covered
- Base defense vs. perimeter vs. rotation
- Reading behind the block
- Age-appropriate defensive schemes for 14U
- Blocker-digger coordination
