## Summary

Jim Stone's full 150-minute practice plan oriented around transitions. Full menu: Neville progressive shuttle warmup; Barnard dig-tip 2v2; Launiere 4v4 Kamikaze; Seemann 5-Before-3 serve-pass; Reimer Coach-on-a-Box transition; Rose hitter-coverage adjustments + coverage drill; Dunning Side-and-Deep serve; Kiraly Xena Warrior team drill. Post-practice: dig-to-swing stats, transition video review, next-day priority chaining.

## Key claims / ideas

- Practice tempo varies from high → lower → high to model match arousal.
- Don't stop-and-talk during drills; pull players aside instead.
- Consecutive-day emphasis is required to move a metric (transition attack efficiency).
- Post-practice video review of transition approaches directly drives next-day practice design.
