## Summary

Ray Bechard (Kansas, 1998-2024) walks through which statistics he tracks as most important for team success, how to take stats in practice, and how to analyze them to inform in-game decisions.

## Key claims / ideas

- Not all statistics are equally predictive of winning — a narrow set matters most.
- Practice stat collection, not just match stat collection, drives coaching decisions.
- Stat analysis is an interpretation skill separate from stat collection.
- Game-situation use of stats is distinct from season-long trend analysis.

## Topics covered

Core stat selection; practice stat collection methodology; stat interpretation; game application of stats.
