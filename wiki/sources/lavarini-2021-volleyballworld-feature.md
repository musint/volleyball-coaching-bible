# Lavarini Infuses New Energy to Korean Programme — Volleyball World (2021)

## Summary
Federation-published feature on Stefano Lavarini's transformation of the South Korea women's NT program ahead of and during the Tokyo 2020 Olympics. Anchors three key claims for the [[korean-school]] page: (1) the first foreign head coach decision, (2) the Italian-school methodology import via Lavarini, (3) Lavarini's explicit framing of "blending Italian volleyball culture with Korean tradition" — the cleanest source quote available for the modern Korean-school identity.

## Key claims / ideas
- Lavarini hired summer 2019; **first foreign head coach** of Korean women's NT
- Result delta: 2019 VNL 15th → 2019 World Cup 6th → Tokyo 2020 4th
- Tokyo 2020 path included a **3-2 quarterfinal upset of 4th-ranked Turkey**
- Lavarini quote: *"I think my staff and I created a good volleyball culture inside the team and infused it with the Korean tradition."*
- Lavarini on Korea's adaptation imperative: *"In Europe, USA and South America, it's very physical volleyball with emphasis on power, height and speed. So we have to create strategies to face the opposition."*
- Lavarini on Kim Yeon-koung: *"Kim is all in one. She's one of the best outside hitters in the world and also a really charismatic leader."*
- Methodology framing: psychological approach over technical overhaul; team-puzzle metaphor

## Topics covered
- Italian-school transfer to Korean NT context
- Stature-disadvantage strategy at international level
- Captain leadership and Lavarini's relationship with Kim Yeon-koung
