## Summary

Karch Kiraly (USA Women's National Team head coach, 2012-2024) names five keys to practice productivity: well-organized plan, emphasis on goals, feedback, focus, and willingness to tackle difficult material.

## Key claims / ideas

- Practice productivity is compound — plan + goals + feedback + focus + difficulty willingness.
- "Tackle the tough stuff" is a practice design choice, not a temperament.
- Feedback is on the top-5 list — it's not optional.
