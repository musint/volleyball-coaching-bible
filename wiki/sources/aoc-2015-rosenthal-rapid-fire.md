## Summary

Six-on-six-style transition + coverage drill from Rosenthal (Lipscomb). Coach enters free ball; after the attack, coach immediately enters a coverage ball; team transitions to attack again. Rotate new group in every ~3 balls.

## Key claims / ideas

- Back-to-back free balls into coverage balls simulate the rally rhythm without live opposition.
- Short rotations keep tempo up and reps distributed.
