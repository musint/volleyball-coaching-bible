# Russ Rose: Game planning – start with your best servers

## Summary
Rose advocates starting the match with your best servers stacked in rotations one, two, and three to maximize their serving opportunities and build an early lead — even if it means violating conventional "opposites" positioning.

## Key claims / ideas
- Load rotations 1, 2, and 3 with your best servers so they serve the most.
- "Don't worry if you have two outside hitters serving (in the first and second rotation). If you can win by serving, don't get caught up in saying, 'They have to be opposite each other.'"
- Bottom line: give your team the best chance to win; serving is a big part of that.
- If best-servers-first lets you build a big lead, do it — don't let rotational convention stop you.

## Topics covered
- Lineup construction around serving strength
- Match-start strategy
- Rotation ordering (when to break the "opposites" heuristic)
