# Training your libero with Karch Kiraly

## Summary

AoC-published segment featuring then-USA WNT head coach Karch Kiraly with assistant Luka Slabe walking through five libero/back-row drills, published 2024-07-02. The companion AoC article is public; the YouTube video itself was not transcript-scrapeable via WebFetch. Content captured is from AoC's written summary. Credibility is high (active NT head coach + AoC editorial), but platform policy keeps this at trust-tier 3 until we corroborate against Tier 1/2 sources (e.g., AVCA/USAV materials).

## Key claims / ideas

- **Blitz the Bro:** 6v6 game with servers deliberately targeting the libero; Kiraly says USAWNT runs this weekly.
- **Fire Drill:** 6v6 with rotation after every point and half-speed attacks. Kiraly on purpose: "The main point of this drill is giving the players some empathy for how hard it is to play other positions."
- **Neville's Pepper Drill:** Points for successful digs and rally wins; all attacks originate from the back row. Can be modified to target zone 5 (libero).
- **Setter-Rotation Game:** If the setter takes the first ball, the libero sets.
- **Game-Planning Exercise:** Libero learns to coordinate position with the blocking line.
- Slabe: drill modifications should always reward quality defensive play.
