# Drill to expand your serve receive range

## Summary
Diane Flick-Williams designs a libero range-expansion drill: one server, one libero in middle back, scored 0-3. Bonus points for perfect passes on sideline balls (+2) or balls outside her body (+1). Race to 15.

## Key claims / ideas
- Libero passes middle back against one server
- Base scoring: 3/2/1 for perfect/mediocre/poor pass
- Bonus +2 for perfect pass on a sideline ball
- Bonus +1 for perfect pass on a ball outside her body
- Race to 15 total points
- Variations: shrink lateral range for younger players, expand to short court / deep corners for advanced

- Libero range expansion
- Bonus-scored serve-receive drill
- Sideline and out-of-body passing
- Pass rating gamified
