# Mike Hebert — University of Illinois Athletics Hall of Fame Page (2023)

## Summary
Official University of Illinois Athletics Hall of Fame page for Mike Hebert, inducted in 2023 (posthumously, four years after his death). Tier 1 as a primary publisher source for the Illinois tenure. Confirms Illinois dates, record, Big Ten title count, Final Four appearances, and the 1988 No. 1 ranking milestone.

## Key claims / ideas
- Illinois Hall of Fame induction year: 2023
- Illinois tenure 1983–1995 with a 323–127 record over 13 seasons
- Four Big Ten titles and two NCAA Final Four appearances at Illinois
- 1988 Illinois team was the first volleyball team east of the Mississippi River to be rated No. 1 nationally
- AVCA National Coach of the Year 1985 (at Illinois); AVCA President 1985–1988

- Illinois-era record and championships
- Early national-contender status of 1980s Illinois women's volleyball
- AVCA president service dates (1985–1988 per this source)
