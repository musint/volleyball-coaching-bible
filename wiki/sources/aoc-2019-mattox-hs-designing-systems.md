# Designing offensive and defensive systems

## Summary
Tod Mattox walks HS coaches through how to build offensive and defensive systems tailored to roster strengths after skill priorities are set. Frames system design as a deliberate, stepwise process rather than adopting a generic template.

## Key claims / ideas
- System design comes after skill priorities and practice structure are settled.
- Systems should be catered to team strengths and the specific athletes on the roster.
- Mattox decomposes system design into clear steps to reduce the "daunting" feel.
- HS Prep Series context: aimed at the coach prepping for an HS season.

## Topics covered
- Offensive and defensive system design
- Rostering systems to personnel
- HS-level season-prep workflow
