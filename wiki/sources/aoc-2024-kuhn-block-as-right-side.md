# Using the block as a right-side hitter

## Summary
Laura Kuhn outlines three ways a right-side hitter can score off the block — sideways swipe, high-contact top-of-hands, and tip over — and proposes a parameter drill (10 balls each of cross, line, tip, swipe, high-off-hand) to rep all shot types.

## Key claims / ideas
- Right-side attackers score off the block with (a) sideways swipes, (b) high contact off the top, and (c) tips over the top.
- Reps should be forced by parameter: 10 cross, 10 line, 10 tips, 10 swipes, 10 high-off-hand.
- A strong blocker on the other side is required — drill only works with a legit blocker challenge.

## Topics covered
- Right-side / opposite attacking tactics vs. block
- Swipe and tooling technique
- Shot selection drill design (parameter round)
