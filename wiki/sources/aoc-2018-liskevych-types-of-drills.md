## Summary

Terry Liskevych (3x USA Olympic coach) classifies drills into three goal types: repetitive ("do 10 reps"), qualitative ("do 7 out of 10 correct"), and in-a-row ("do 3 correct in a row").

## Key claims / ideas

- Three distinct drill-goal types: repetitive, qualitative, in-a-row.
- Repetitive drills build volume; qualitative drills tolerate some failure; in-a-row drills demand consistency.
- Choice of goal type changes how players mentally engage with the drill.

## Topics covered

Drill-goal taxonomy (repetitive / qualitative / in-a-row); quality thresholds; drill framing.
