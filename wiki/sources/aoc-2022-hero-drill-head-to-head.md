# Hero drill: Head to head position drill

## Summary
A competitive head-to-head drill pitting left-side hitters against servers. After each point, the server and the left-side hitter on the same side swap positions, and the left-side hitter must receive the first set of the next point.

## Key claims / ideas
- Left-side hitters must pass the first ball, tying serve-receive responsibility to primary attackers.
- Server-passer role rotation after each rally keeps both skills under game pressure.
- Game to 10 or 15 points produces sustained competitive stakes.
- Requires at least 6 players and a full court.
