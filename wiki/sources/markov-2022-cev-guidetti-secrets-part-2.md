# Markov (2022) — Secrets of Coaching Success: Guidetti (Part 2, CEV)

## Summary

CEV feature-interview by Nikolay Markov published April 19, 2022, during the closing stages of the 2021–22 Champions League season that VakıfBank won. Part 2 of a two-part series; where Part 1 covers Guidetti's career biography, Part 2 reports his working methodology and coaching philosophy in Guidetti's own words. Primary value: the first-person framing of his recovery-periodization practice and the data-blend triad ("stomach, eyes, heart") that connect his stated method to the peer-reviewed VakıfBank load-management study (Akyildiz et al. 2022).

## Key claims / ideas

- Recovery periodization as deliberate methodology. Direct quote: "To rest is key to the performance so I like to alternate moments of 110% volleyball to moments of 0% volleyball."
- Player autonomy as philosophy. Direct quote: "All the rest, I really don't care! Players take millions of decisions and responsibilities in every match, so why do I have to decide for them outside the gym? Trust is the key."
- Data + observation + intuition blend. Direct quote: "It is a mix of data from the stomach, the eyes and the heart!" — explicit acknowledgement that quantitative data is part of, not the whole of, decision-making.
- Markov attributes broader contextual color about trust-based culture at VakıfBank and leadership philosophy ("Key points of coaching are harmony and stability").

## Topics covered

- Recovery periodization ("110% / 0%" alternation)
- Load management as explicit coaching principle
- Coach-player trust and autonomy model
- Data + observation + intuition blending in decision-making
- Leadership stability and team culture
