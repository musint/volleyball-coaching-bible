## Summary

Coach Connection LIVE session with Mac Barron, founder of Meraki Volleyball, on designing processes for coach development and evaluation inside a club. Covers onboarding to gym culture and expectations, in-season check-ins, data/stats education for coaches, and communication stack choices.

## Key claims / ideas

- Coach evaluation is a club-level system problem, not a head-coach instinct.
- Onboarding must explicitly transfer gym culture and expectations, not just drills.
- Mid-season check-ins with coaches need a timing cadence, not drop-in conversations.
- A single communication app for club coaches reduces friction versus scattered channels.
- Teaching coaches to use stats and data is part of coach development, not a "head coach only" skill.
- Document security across changing staff years is a real club ops concern.

## Topics covered

Coach onboarding; coach evaluation rubrics; communication app selection; document security; stats education for assistants; mission alignment.
