# The best volleyball drills for beginners: 15 fun youth drills

## Summary
Deborah Newkirk walks through 15 short drills from a 30-minute youth practice video covering energy checks, down-ball hitting, setting progressions, low-base passing, serve development, and cooperative ball-control games.

## Key claims / ideas
- Energy-check rituals between drills sustain engagement for beginners.
- Setting progression: toss-up → fizzles (low sets to self) → four sets (low-low-low-high) to build feel.
- Passing progression builds on low base: low catch → burpee → flip-flop rotations.
- "Deliver and Pop" breaks the overhand serve into reach + drop + pop for the contact.
- Cooperative "10 to Kill" gamifies ball control across the net with shared point structure.

## Topics covered
- Beginner-friendly drills across all six skills
- Low-base passing progression
- Energy management in youth practices
- Cooperative vs. competitive ball-control formats
