# The 10 Cult Phrases of Julio Velasco

## Summary

November 2020 MOK Umag (Croatian volleyball club) piece compiling ten "cult phrases" of Julio Velasco in English translation. Used on the Velasco coach page as corroboration for the Lebedew 2020 12-quotes source — the two lists overlap on the "he who wins celebrates / he who loses explains" and "spikers don't talk about the set" canonical lines, confirming the attribution across independent secondary sources.

## Key claims / ideas

- "Whoever wins celebrates, whoever loses explains" — matches Lebedew #8.
- **"Winners find solutions. Losers are looking for alibis"** — the cleanest articulation of Velasco's "find ways not excuses" frame; corroborates the Lebedew "culture of alibis" material.
- "I want attackers who hit bad balls well...solve it [don't talk about it]" — matches Lebedew #7 in substance (translation differs: "attackers" vs "spikers"; "balls" vs "set").
- "Great coach teaches players to move on their own" — coach-as-teacher, corroborates Lebedew #12.
- "If we lose it is the fault of the electrician" — sarcastic Velasco shorthand for deflection of blame, widely circulated.
- "Leader must be fair...hard on everyone" — fairness-as-leadership framing.
