# Drill for setting high, spinning, out-of-system balls

## Summary
Mattox's partner drill for setting high spinning balls: flip-the-wrist high toss creates spin, setter receives in setting window and delivers. 30 ft apart. Trains comfort with the spin-dominated OOS ball that arises from emergency digs.

## Key claims / ideas
- OOS balls often arrive high and spinning; setters must be comfortable with them.
- Drill: pair 30 ft apart; flip-wrist high toss to self-simulate a spinning ball; set to partner.
- Contact in the setting window is the coaching point.
