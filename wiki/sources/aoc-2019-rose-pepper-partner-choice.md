## Summary

Russ Rose: who you pepper with matters. Pair with partners who don't know your tendencies — or who know them but actively challenge them. Often means *not* the best-friend or same-position pairing.

## Key claims / ideas

- Pepper is meant to be a dynamic warmup varying shots, tempos, and competition levels.
- Best-friend pairing usually produces comfortable pepper that doesn't train anything.
- Same-position pairing narrows shot menu.
