## Summary

Bishop's School 4v4 scrimmage. 2 pin attackers + 2 back-row per side. Scoring rules adapt to focus (e.g., cross-court kills only, 2 points for middle-zone kills). Winner stays; loser replaced by waiting team. Incoming team must shout score on entry or get sent off.

## Key claims / ideas

- 4v4 with constrained-swing scoring (cross-court only) trains precision under pressure.
- Shout-your-score entry rule builds team urgency + accountability.
- Winner-stays format creates scoreboard pressure across multiple teams.
