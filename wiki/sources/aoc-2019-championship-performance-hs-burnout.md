## Summary

Mental-training heavyweight Jim Loehr plus sports-psychology and sports-medicine voices on the HS burnout epidemic. "Dream kids" — high-compliance athletes — are most at risk. Physical warning signs include sleep disruption, chronic muscle pain, and appetite changes; mental signs include personality change and lack of enthusiasm.

## Key claims / ideas

- Burnout has no clinical diagnosis but is recognizable via Loehr's pattern (dream-kid profile).
- Coaching/parenting pressure to never take time off is a primary driver.
- Year-round play can benefit elite pathways but raises injury severity risk (Santiso).
- AAP and The Educated Sports Parent recommend breaks between training seasons to avoid injuries and burnout.
- Recovery-mechanism failure (sleep, appetite) signals overtraining syndrome.
