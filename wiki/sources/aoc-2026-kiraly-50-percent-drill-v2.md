## Summary

Karch Kiraly's reworked "50% drill" (a 2026 video walkthrough; companion to the 2024 AOC post). 6v6 with the right-back player as setter so everyone cycles through every position. Coach enters the ball. Start the drill at 12-all in a game to 15. One constraint: no one can hit harder than 50% velocity. Winners serve next point. The half-speed cap forces long rallies and creative offense — players must think, fool the defense, and use placement over power.

## Key claims / ideas

- Half-speed hitting generates long rallies because pure power is off the table.
- Offensive creativity is the trained outcome: tips, off-speed, shot placement, setter misdirection.
- Right-back setter keeps the setter in back row, which also means every player gets reps setting.
- Starting at 12-all focuses every rep on late-game pressure without playing full games.
- This is an update to the original 50% drill (see companion 2024 article).
