# Karch Kiraly to Coach U.S. Men's National Team

## Summary

This is USA Volleyball's own October 24, 2024 announcement that Karch Kiraly would move from head coach of the U.S. Women's National Team (a role held since September 2012) to head coach of the U.S. Men's National Team for the 2028 Olympic & Paralympic cycle. Because it is hosted on usavolleyball.org and carries the federation's imprimatur, it is the primary-source citation for the 2024 transition itself; secondary coverage (VolleyballMag.com, ESPN, Volleyball World) should be used only as supplements.

The announcement also restates Kiraly's women's tenure resume: 327-81 record; Olympic silver (Paris 2024), gold (Tokyo 2020), bronze (Rio 2016); the 2014 World Championship; three Volleyball Nations League titles. It includes quotes from both John Speraw (incoming USAV President/CEO and outgoing men's head coach) and Kiraly himself.

## Key claims / ideas

- Appointment announced October 24, 2024; role: U.S. Men's National Team head coach for LA 2028 cycle.
- Kiraly replaces Speraw, who became USA Volleyball's president and CEO on October 1, 2024.
- Speraw on Kiraly: "I have complete and total trust in Karch's process. He has a relentless work ethic, brilliant intellect..."
- Kiraly on the move: "For one-quarter of my life, I've poured everything I have into the U.S. Women's Team..."
- Kiraly on the men's program: "I've watched with respect and admiration for everyone in the program as they've pursued excellence..."
- Women's NT coaching resume reaffirmed: 327-81; Olympic medals of all three colors; 2014 Worlds; three VNL titles.
