# Stone — Left Back: A Great Home for Your Libero (AOC, 2017)

## Summary

A canonical AOC piece advocating libero-at-LB as the default modern women's-game defensive base. Jim Stone — longtime collegiate coach (Ohio State head coach 1985-2010, USA NT staff) — frames the LB position as the single mental-model home for the libero across both serve-receive (line passing) and defense (line-cut deep reading).

## Key claims / ideas

- **LB is the "great home" for the libero** — single base position for both phases (serve-receive and defense).
- **Cross-court OH attack lands in zone 5** — putting the libero on the line aligns the team's best defender with the most-attacked zone.
- **Single mental model** for the libero — line-passing in serve-receive + line-cut deep reading in defense — reduces cognitive load relative to schemes that have her base in different zones for the two phases.
- **Setter-coverage and out-of-system second-contact angles favor LB** — closer to OH cover.
- **Pairs with read-defense identity** rather than landmark-anchor positioning.
