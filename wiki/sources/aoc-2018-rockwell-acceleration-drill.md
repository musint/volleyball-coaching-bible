## Summary

Defensive drill from Rockwell (2015 Vancouver Clinic footage): three coaches feed quick balls and the defender must accelerate through contact. Emphasis on forward momentum through the dig rather than catching balls flat-footed.

## Key claims / ideas

- Defenders must accelerate through the ball, not wait for it.
- Multi-coach feeding increases rep density and forces faster recovery between touches.
