## Summary

Russ Rose (Penn State '79-'21) walks through how to design drills based on what you're trying to work on and how to dial difficulty up or down for different team levels.

## Key claims / ideas

- Drill setup is a function of the learning target, not a catalog you pick from.
- Same drill concept at different levels requires different parameters — not just "the same drill, slower."

## Topics covered

Drill design from learning targets; level-adjustment of drill parameters.
