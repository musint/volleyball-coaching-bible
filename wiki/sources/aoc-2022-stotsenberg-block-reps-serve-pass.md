# Blocking drill: Reps off a serve and pass

## Summary
Wave Volleyball's Jed Stotsenberg runs a high-rep blocking drill: three blockers face a serve-receive team with hitters at all three positions, blockers try to stuff or touch but do not transition — maximizing blocker reps.

## Key claims / ideas
- Blockers do NOT transition to swing — drill stays in blocking focus.
- Serve starts play; coach can insert balls when servers miss to keep pace.
- Coach teaching points: hand placement, pin-blocker positioning, lining up against hitters.
- Reading hitters is a key coaching cue throughout.

## Topics covered
- High-rep blocking drill from serve-receive.
- Coaching points for pin blocker positioning.
- Reading attackers.
