# What I learned from Russ Rose

## Summary
Short video intro featuring Penn State All-American Christa Dietzen on how Rose's Penn State program treated team leaders: responsible for teammates' actions, no excuses, leading by the way you play, practice, and live.

## Key claims / ideas
- Excuses are not a big part of Rose's program at Penn State.
- Team leaders are responsible for the actions of their teammates.
- Leaders must play, practice, and live in a way that benefits the program.
- Leadership is a thorough, full-time responsibility — extending beyond the court.

## Topics covered
- Leadership accountability at Penn State
- No-excuses culture
- Player-leader responsibility model
