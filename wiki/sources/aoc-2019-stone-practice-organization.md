## Summary

Jim Stone breaks down the components of a productive practice: pre-planning, drill selection, skill development, integration of competition, and after-practice evaluation. Part of AOC's HS Prep Series.

- Pre-planning is the differentiator between productive and reactive practice.
- Post-practice evaluation is a coach discipline, not a bonus.
