# Growing a sport: Terry Liskevych on boosting volleyball's popularity

## Summary
Leave It Better Podcast episode with Janice Kruger (Side-Out Foundation). Liskevych reviews the work to build volleyball's popularity in the US, critiques USA Volleyball governance, and argues that players — not coaches — drive the sport forward.

## Key claims / ideas
- Early US volleyball development focused on visibility and coach-development infrastructure.
- Liskevych voices skepticism about current USA Volleyball organizational leadership.
- In the perennial players-vs-coaches debate, he picks players as the scarcer, more decisive resource.
- Coaches build frameworks; players create the product fans follow.

## Topics covered
- History of US volleyball development
- USA Volleyball governance critique
- Players vs. coaches as drivers of sport growth
- Side-Out Foundation and Dig Pink
