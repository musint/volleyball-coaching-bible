# Karch Kiraly on training more efficiently: Making the minutes count

## Summary
Kiraly and Luka Slabe discuss how to squeeze more out of every practice minute: replacing non-volleyball warmups with volleyball-specific movement, using 6v6 activities, and choosing random over blocked practice.

## Key claims / ideas
- Warmups should be volleyball-specific movements, not generic conditioning.
- 6v6 activity is a high-yield format when practice minutes are scarce.
- Random practice beats blocked practice for retention and transfer.
- Practice-time efficiency is a lever every coach controls at every level.

## Topics covered
- Practice planning and efficiency
- Volleyball-specific warmups
- Random vs. blocked practice
- 6v6 as training format
