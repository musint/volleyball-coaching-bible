## Summary

Six-on-six awareness and communication drill. Coach tosses to the offensive side and shouts "1", "2", or "3" indicating how many contacts are left before the ball must cross the net. Rotating-side scoring enforces pressure; coaching emphasis is on who sets when setter takes ball 1, which hitters are available, and committing to swing on the third contact.

## Key claims / ideas

- Constrained-contact toss drills force communication under pressure.
- Rotation after 2 points keeps reps fair and prevents fatigue favoring one side.
- The variation explicitly trains third-contact commitment (swing, don't send it soft).
