# Fédération Française de Volley (Wikipedia)

## Summary

French-Wikipedia reference for the **Fédération Française de Volley (FFVolley)**, the governing body of volleyball in France. Founded 2 February 1936 as FFVB, rebranded FFVolley in 2017. Founding member of the FIVB; member of the CEV. Manages French men's and women's pro-league competition under the Ligue Nationale de Volley umbrella plus lower-tier divisions, plus national team programs.

## Key claims / ideas

- Founded **2 February 1936**.
- Official current name: **FFVolley** (rebranded in 2017 from FFVB).
- Headquarters: 2-4 Rue des Sarrazins, 94000 Créteil (Paris suburb).
- Current president: **Éric Tanguy** (since September 2015).
- Membership (2024): 1,311 clubs, 155,635 licensed members.
- **Founding member of the FIVB.** Affiliated with FIVB and CEV.
- Manages: Ligue A men (14 pro clubs), Ligue A women (12 pro clubs), Ligue B, Élite, Nationale levels.
- Operates Centre national de volley-ball and Institut fédéral de volley-ball.
