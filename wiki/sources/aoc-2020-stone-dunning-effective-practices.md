## Summary

AOC 2020 Virtual Clinic session with Jim Stone (USA Girls Youth National Team) and John Dunning (Stanford '01-'16) on error detection, skill correction, and proper use of drills to drive team and individual improvement.

## Key claims / ideas

- Effective practice is built on three separable skills: error detection, skill correction, and drill selection.
- Drill selection should follow detected errors, not precede them.
- Team improvement and individual improvement are different optimization problems.
- Virtual-clinic Q&A format surfaces coach decision criteria that books and videos often omit.

## Topics covered

Error detection; skill correction; drill-selection methodology; coach Q&A format.
