# 180 shuttle drill

## Summary
Tod Mattox's 180 Shuttle: toss over net, receiver passes to herself, spins 180°, bumps back over. Build a rally; miss = rotate out. Trains ball control + spatial awareness.

## Key claims / ideas
- Sequence: toss over net → pass to self → 180° spin → bump back over
- Goal: build a continuous rally between two players
- Miss (ball hits floor) = rotate out, next player in
- Trains spatial awareness and ball control in one drill
- Quick, low-setup drill

## Topics covered
- Self-pass ball control
- 180° body-orientation drill
- Rally-chaining discipline
