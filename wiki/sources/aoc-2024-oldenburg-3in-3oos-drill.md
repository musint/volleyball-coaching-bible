# Jen Flynn Oldenburg's 3 in-system, 3 out-of-system drill

## Summary
Ohio State drill that alternates in-system (coach free-ball) and out-of-system (coach bounces ball deep, team has 2 touches) opportunities. 3 front + 1 back defenders vs. 6 offensive players. Team on offense tries for 6 in a row. Emphasis: hitters take rips, OOS setters (setter or not) "go for it" and are bold.

## Key claims / ideas
- Alternating in-system/OOS reps recreates match-like swings in rally quality.
- Setup: 3 FR + 1 BR defenders vs. 6 offense; free-ball initiates IS; coach-bounce deep initiates OOS (two touches).
- OOS settable-by-anyone is a team skill — not just the setter.
- "Hitters take rips" → OOS setters must be BOLD with contact.
- Target: 6 offensive points in a row.

## Topics covered
- In-system vs. out-of-system alternation
- Non-setter OOS setting
- Bold contact on OOS sets
- Team-scale drill design
