# 5 vs. 5 drill to work on defending a hole in the block

## Summary
Russ Rose's 5v5 (each side missing a middle) forces single-blocks and makes diggers defend a deliberate hole; first to 5; defenders must line up precisely with the opposite hitter's shoulder and stay put.

## Key claims / ideas
- Remove the middle blocker on both sides to force single-blocks.
- Two coaches initiate; play out normally.
- Defenders line up with hitter's shoulder, not the ball.
- First to 5 points — quick, high-pressure reps.

## Topics covered
- Defending around a deliberate block hole.
- Shoulder-line digger alignment.
- Single-block only format.
