# Blocking: How to shut down the slide

## Summary
John Dunning drills middle blockers to swing-block left against the slide, with an optional setter choice variation to force reading between slide and middle.

## Key claims / ideas
- Middle blockers poised to swing-block left to stop the slide.
- Hitters run slides one at a time; timing is essential.
- Variation: setter chooses slide or middle, so blocker must read.
- Explicit slide-shutdown rep pattern.

## Topics covered
- Swing blocking the slide.
- Middle-vs-slide read variation.
- Timing on slide block.
