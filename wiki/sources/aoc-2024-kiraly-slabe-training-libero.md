# Training your libero with Karch Kiraly

## Summary
Karch Kiraly and Luka Slabe catalog libero drills used by USA WNT: Blitz the Bro (target server), Fire Drill (rotate every position), Neville's Pepper (dig reward / no-pursuit penalty), libero-as-setter, and block-libero coordination.

## Key claims / ideas
- "Blitz the Bro": 6v6 with servers targeting the libero; USA uses weekly.
- "Fire Drill": 6v6 with rotation every point; builds empathy across positions.
- "Neville's Pepper": point for each dig + rally win; -1 for dig-over-net; lose all if you don't pursue.
- Modify Neville's to send first attacks to Zone 5 (libero) for more reps.
- Libero-sets-when-setter-takes-first-ball game.
- Final drill: libero/block coordination game-plan exercise.
