# Middle school volleyball warmups: Short ball long ball drill

## Summary
Mike Lingenfelter's (Munciana) youth warmup: three passers rotate through short-long-short ball feeds from a coach. Fast-moving pursuit drill targeting 15-20 solid passes before rest.

## Key claims / ideas
- Three passers: two on the 10-foot line, one middle back
- Coach feeds rapid-fire: short ball to right-side, long down the line to MB, short to left-side
- After passing, 10-foot players rotate to middle back; MB passer replaces short-ball passer
- Target: 15-20 solid passes before allowing rest
- Great warmup for middle school / beginner teams
