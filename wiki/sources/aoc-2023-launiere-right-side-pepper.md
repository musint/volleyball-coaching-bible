# Right-side hitting drill with over-the-net pepper format

## Summary
Utah's Beth Launiere modifies over-the-net pepper into a right-side-only 3v3 format so opposites get concentrated cross-court swing reps, with a line-swing variation and optional target-based competitive scoring.

## Key claims / ideas
- 3v3 setup per side: hitter (right front), setter (net), passer (right back).
- Every set must go to Zone 2, every swing is cross-court — high rep density for opposites.
- Rotate roles clockwise or counterclockwise after each rally.
- Line-swing variation: move the passer to the left side, require the attacker to hit line.
- Competitive overlay: place court markers and award points for hitting target zones.

## Topics covered
- Right-side-only cooperative hitting rep structure
- Cross-court vs line attack variations
- Role rotation mechanics
- Target-zone competitive scoring
