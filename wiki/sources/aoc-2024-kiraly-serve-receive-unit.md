# Karch Kiraly on developing your serve receive unit

## Summary
Kiraly and Luka Slabe lay out a four-question framework for building a serve-receive unit: technique, system and passer responsibilities, tactics (number of receivers, platform vs. hands), and creating gamelike SR opportunities.

## Key claims / ideas
- Pick one technique for the gym and teach it consistently.
- Define the system and each passer's zone/responsibility.
- Tactics lever: 2-, 3-, or more-receiver patterns; platform-only vs. hands-allowed decisions.
- Maximize gamelike serve-receive reps in practice.

## Topics covered
- Serve-receive system design
- Passer responsibility mapping
- 2/3-person receive tactics
- Platform vs. hands in reception
- Gamelike practice design for SR
