# Osvaldo Idel Martínez & Mireya Luis elected to lead Cuban Volleyball Federation — FIVB

## Summary
FIVB official news article (May 2025) reporting the FCV General Assembly that elected the federation's 2025–2028 leadership cycle: **Osvaldo Idel Martínez Arias** as president and **[[mireya-luis]] Hernández** as vice president. The article is the cleanest English-language anchor for current FCV governance, executive committee composition (Yumilka Ruiz as National Commissioner, Ricardo Borroto Secretary General, Lorenzo Martínez Treasurer), and the FCV's NORCECA-jurisdiction / FIVB-affiliation context. Used as the primary source on the [[fcv-cuba]] federation stub.

## Key claims / ideas
- General Assembly held in **Havana, May 9, 2025** for the **2025–2028** federation cycle.
- **Osvaldo Idel Martínez Arias** elected FCV president (previously Secretary General; 40+ years of Cuban-volleyball experience).
- **Mireya Luis Hernández** elected FCV vice president — concurrent with her **FIVB Executive Vice President** role.
- Executive Committee: Yumilka Ruiz (Board / National Commissioner); Ricardo Borroto (Secretary General); Lorenzo Martínez (Treasurer); Jorge Sosa, Jorge Luis García (Board Members).
- FCV operates within NORCECA jurisdiction; FIVB-affiliated.
- General Assembly presided over by Cuban Olympic Committee President **Roberto León Richards** and Sports Minister **Osvaldo Vento Montiller**.
- Article does NOT document FCV founding date, headquarters, club counts, or comprehensive presidency chronology.
