# Vôlei: Aprendendo a Jogar + Pensar Bem... Sai Bem — Bernardinho

## Summary

This citation key covers **two earlier Bernardinho books** that predate
his 2006 leadership volume *Transformando Suor em Ouro* (already
ingested separately as `bernardinho-2006-transformando-suor-em-ouro`).
The two books occupy different genres and are bundled here because they
share a publication context — Bernardinho's late-1990s / early-2000s
Brazilian-public profile after taking over the Brazil men's national
team in 2001.

- **Vôlei: Aprendendo a Jogar** ("Volleyball: Learning to Play"). A
  technique-instruction book aimed at Brazilian beginning-to-
  intermediate players and youth coaches. Brazilian counterpart to USA
  *Volleyball: Steps to Success* (Pauley) format — basic
  passing/setting/hitting/serving progressions, court positions,
  introductory 6-2/5-1/4-2 systems, youth-coaching priorities. Specific
  publisher, ISBN, year [unsourced] pending Brazilian-retailer fetch.
- **Pensar Bem... Sai Bem** ("Think Well... Come Out Well"). A shorter
  leadership/mindset volume in the Brazilian motivational-business
  genre, predating *Transformando Suor em Ouro*. Title encodes the
  Bernardinho through-line that mental preparation drives competitive
  performance — "thinking well" → "coming out well" in pressure
  moments.

The wiki's existing Bernardinho sourcing emphasizes the 2006 leadership
book + the IVHF / Wikipedia biographical capture. These earlier titles
add Bernardinho's **technique-instruction voice** (*Vôlei: Aprendendo
a Jogar*) and his **early leadership voice** (*Pensar Bem... Sai Bem*),
useful for technique-page and mental-page citation backfill that the
2006 framework book does not cover at the same operational specificity.

### Vôlei: Aprendendo a Jogar (technique manual)

- **Brazilian fundamental-skills sequencing.** The Brazilian men's
  program of the 1990s–2000s prioritized fundamentals (passing, setting,
  defensive footwork) before tactical complexity. The book is expected
  to articulate that progression for youth coaches. [unsourced]
- **Speed-first offensive tempo.** The Brazilian-school identity pillar
  per [[brazilian-school]] — faster middle-attack tempo than other
  national programs. Beginner-level introduction to tempo systems is
  expected here. [unsourced]
- **6-2 and 5-1 system introductions.** Standard youth-volleyball
  curriculum structure for Brazilian club coaches in the 2000s.
  [unsourced]
- **Position-skill expectations.** OH/MB/setter/libero role
  introductions at the youth level. [unsourced]

### Pensar Bem... Sai Bem (leadership/mindset)

- **Mental preparation precedes physical execution.** Title encodes the
  through-line; book serves as a precursor to the Wheel-of-Excellence
  pressure-management dimension formalized in *Transformando Suor em
  Ouro* (2006). [unsourced]
- **Pressure as trainable, not temperamental.** Composure under
  match-point conditions framed as a discipline rather than a
  character trait — same framing the 2006 book formalizes. [unsourced]
- **Athlete mindset for non-athletes.** Read in Brazilian business
  contexts as a leadership primer aimed beyond the volleyball
  audience — precursor to the broader cross-domain framing of
  *Transformando Suor em Ouro*. [unsourced]
- **Commitment, complicity, discipline.** The three-word team-culture
  shorthand documented in the 2006 book likely originates or is
  prefigured in *Pensar Bem... Sai Bem*. [unsourced]
