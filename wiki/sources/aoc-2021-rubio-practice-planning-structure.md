## Summary

Dave Rubio (Arizona, 1992-2022) — a former educator — describes how he builds per-practice spreadsheets assigning drills to individual players. Each practice has a dominant focus: fundamentals, systems, out-of-system, position training, or down-ball training.

## Key claims / ideas

- Per-player drill assignment turns generic practice into individualized practice.
- A practice should have a dominant focus rather than a balanced sample of everything.
- Named focus categories (fundamentals, systems, out-of-system, position training, down-ball) give structure to weekly rotation.
- Drill efficiency and player engagement-at-all-times are measurable success criteria.
- Practice progression across the season requires different focus mixes at different phases.
