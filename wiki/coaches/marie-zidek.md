# Marie Zidek

## Overview

Marie Zidek is the head coach of DePaul University women's volleyball and a sustained Art of Coaching Volleyball (AOC) contributor whose public footprint in the ingested sample is concentrated on dynamic warm-up design, defensive shifts, and out-of-system drills [aoc-2019-zidek-10-warmup-exercises] [aoc-2021-zidek-20-dynamic-warmups] [aoc-2019-zidek-defensive-shifts-tips] [aoc-2024-zidek-oos-drills]. Zidek's AOC output shares a throughline: warm-ups should be volleyball-specific movement-preparation (not generic S&C) and should prime the specific defensive, transition, and attack patterns the team will rep during the skill block [aoc-2019-zidek-10-warmup-exercises] [aoc-2021-zidek-20-dynamic-warmups]. ## Core teaching principles

**Volleyball-specific dynamic warm-up.** Zidek's 2019 and 2021 warm-up pieces argue that warm-ups should replicate volleyball movement patterns — lateral cuts, jump-preparation, approach footwork — rather than generic S&C activation [aoc-2019-zidek-10-warmup-exercises] [aoc-2021-zidek-20-dynamic-warmups].

**Defensive shifts as a pre-contact skill.** The 2019 defensive-shifts piece treats defensive positioning as a pre-contact read problem — defenders move before the attacker contacts the ball based on the block and the hitter's approach — rather than a reactive skill [aoc-2019-zidek-defensive-shifts-tips].

**OOS work as its own skill set.** Zidek's 2024 OOS pieces treat out-of-system play as a distinct trainable domain with drill-specific demands separate from in-system reps [aoc-2024-zidek-oos-drills].

## Contributions to the game

**Dynamic-warmup library.** Zidek's warm-up pieces contribute a ready-to-use library of volleyball-specific warm-up movements that other head coaches adapt to their own practice structures [aoc-2019-zidek-10-warmup-exercises] [aoc-2021-zidek-20-dynamic-warmups].

**Injury-prevention-adjacent practice design.** The volleyball-specific movement-preparation frame is cited on physical-preparation and warm-up pages as an approach that reduces non-contact injury risk while preserving sport-specificity [aoc-2021-zidek-20-dynamic-warmups] [unsourced].
