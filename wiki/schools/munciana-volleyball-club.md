# Munciana Volleyball Club

## Overview
Stub for a club / program school page — will be fleshed out in a later wave. This page exists to satisfy the SCHEMA §6 bidirectional-link invariant between [[mike-lingenfelter]]'s `schools:` frontmatter (which lists `munciana-volleyball-club`) and an actual `wiki/schools/munciana-volleyball-club.md` file. Munciana Volleyball Club is a nationally-recognized Indiana-based girls' club program headquartered in Muncie, Indiana, co-directed by Alyssa and Mike Lingenfelter. Munciana's distinguishing program choice is **club-wide system alignment** — a single unified skill-training and systems framework that applies from the youngest teams through 18U so players never have to relearn technique or rotations as they move up age groups [aoc-2025-cc-skill-training-clubwide]. The program's public coaching voice comes through [[art-of-coaching-volleyball]] via Mike Lingenfelter's video series on middle-attack tempo, accountability-driven passing, and youth warmup design [aoc-2026-lingenfelter-middle-attack-tempo] [aoc-2026-lingenfelter-accountability-passing] [aoc-2021-lingenfelter-short-ball-long-ball].
