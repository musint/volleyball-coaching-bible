The Chinese volleyball tradition — 排球精神 ("volleyball spirit") — is the high-discipline, high-repetition, collective-grit national pedagogy that produced the China Women's NT gold-medal runs of 1984 (Los Angeles) and 2016 (Rio). Lang Ping's dual-tradition framing — "spirit alone will not bring victories; we also need scientific training and management systems" — is the canonical articulation of how the tradition is being modernized in place rather than discarded.
