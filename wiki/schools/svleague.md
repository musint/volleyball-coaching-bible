SV.League (launched October 2024) is Japan's top-tier professional men's and women's volleyball league, succeeding the semi-professional V.League. The league represents a structural shift in Japanese volleyball from an industrial-team / semi-pro model to a fully professional franchise model intended to raise competitive parity with the Italian, Turkish, and Brazilian professional circuits.
