# Pass-Set-Hit

## Setup
Full court, one side. Three-person unit on the receiving side: a passer in the back court, a setter at target, and an attacker at an outside pin (start left-side). Coach or servers on the opposite endline with a ball cart. Shaggers roll balls back to the cart.

## Execution
1. Coach tosses or serves into the passer's seam.
2. Passer delivers a forearm pass to the setter's target window.
3. Setter delivers a front-row set (high ball for 14U, tempo for HS/college).
4. Attacker completes a full approach and attacks over the net.
5. Unit rotates after 5-7 reps, then moves to the next attacker position (middle, then right).
6. Grade each rep on three criteria: pass target (0-3), set location, attack result.

## Coaching points
- Passer focuses on delivering a hittable ball — the test is whether the setter has options.
- Setter shows hands early and calls the attacker's number.
- Attacker starts the approach off the passer's platform angle, not off the set — trains anticipation.
- Coach holds tempo consistent; the drill collapses when feed quality drifts.

## Variations
- **Live serve:** swap the coach for a live server; count only cleanly completed points.
- **Three-position rotation:** 5 reps outside, 5 middle, 5 right; track kill percentage per position.
- **Wash format:** scoring team needs two consecutive terminations to "wash" and earn a point (see [[serve-receive-3v3]]).

## Adaptations by level
- **14U:** coach tosses only, outside high balls, score on attack location not termination.
- **HS:** live serving, outside and middle options, hittable-set and kill percentage tracked.
- **College:** jump-float/topspin serving, full in-system including slide and back-row, per-rotation stats.
