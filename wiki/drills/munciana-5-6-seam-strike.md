# Munciana 5-6 Seam Strike (Box on the Fly)

## Setup
Place a ball box (or an upright mat / large hard target) on the floor in the 5-6 seam — between zones 5 and 6, deep-left and middle-deep on the receiving side. Servers behind the endline with a ball cart. Target stands clear of the box. Two shaggers reset balls.

## Execution
1. Servers cycle one at a time and aim to land the served ball directly into the box on the fly — no bounce.
2. **[00:27:53]** "Generally do that drill until someone gets five. Someone has to hit the box on the fly five times."
3. Servers track personal hit count aloud or on a whiteboard.
4. Move the box to a new seam or zone at the start of each block.

## Coaching points
- "It's like basketball. If [the ball] all goes in, it's good. If it doesn't, it's not." **[00:27:20]** Binary scoring — no half-credit for "close."
- Serve with purpose. **[00:27:27]** "We don't want to serve randomly. We want to serve with purpose in everything we do."
- Pair the noodle for trajectory ceiling and the box for landing point. The two together define the entire serve geometry.
- If you don't have a box, use a mat, towel, or any defined object — **[00:27:31]** "We've got to give them targets to serve at constantly."

## Variations
- Two-target stack: place a box in 5 and a box in 1; servers alternate.
- Earn-back: only servers who hit a box on the fly stay; misses rotate to the back of the line.
- Lower the bar to three hits for tighter time blocks.

## Adaptations by level
- **14U**: target box plus a 2 m radius around it counts; four hits to clear.
- **HS**: box-only; five hits to clear.
- **College**: box-only across all six receive zones; five hits while alternating zones each rep.
