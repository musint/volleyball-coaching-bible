# Munciana 5-6 Seam (Noodle Aim-Small)

## Setup
Servers behind the endline with a ball cart. A held noodle on the receiving side sits about 18 inches above the net tape, positioned in the 5-6 seam (between zones 5 and 6 on the receive side — the deep left and middle-deep). Move the noodle left, right, or to the 1-6 seam to retarget. One holder stays alert; a second player shags balls back to the cart.

## Execution
1. Coach names the seam; servers cycle one at a time and aim the serve to pass directly over the noodle.
2. **[00:25:57]** "We will generally do this until three noodles are hit." Run until three direct hits, then move on or rotate the noodle.
3. Misses go in the cart; no consequence beyond "do it again."
4. Pair this directly into [[munciana-5-6-seam-strike]] for the box-on-the-fly progression.

## Coaching points
- Aim small, miss small. **[00:25:23]** "One of the biggest things with serving is aim small, miss small." A two-inch by two-inch target replaces "900 square feet, nice serve."
- **[00:26:26]** "All too often in serving we just serve to serve, and it becomes somewhat of a dumpster fire." Hold servers accountable to a finite target every rep.
- The noodle teaches trajectory ceiling: serves that clear by inches travel flat and fast, not lobby. Aim above the tape but not by much.
- Servers always serve with purpose. A purposeless serve is a wasted rep.

## Variations
- Move noodle to 1-6 seam (deep right / middle-deep) for cross-side accountability.
- Stack three noodles vertically along the tape and require the server to clear the middle one without touching the outer two.

## Adaptations by level
- **14U**: hold the noodle at 24 inches above the tape; success threshold = one direct hit per server.
- **HS**: 18 inches; three direct hits before moving on.
- **College**: 12 inches and rotate noodle holders into different seams every rep so the target moves with each toss.
