# Two-Line Passing

## Setup
Two lines of passers in the deep corners of one side (around the 5 m line, left-back and right-back). A single target/setter at the target area. A coach/tosser at the opposite endline or inside the net on the opposite side with a ball cart. Shaggers rotate balls back to the cart.

## Execution
1. Lead passer in line A steps into the back-court seam.
2. Coach tosses or serves a ball to that passer's platform.
3. Passer delivers a forearm pass to the target; target grades aloud and catches.
4. Passer shags her own ball back to the cart and returns to the tail of line B.
5. Lead passer in line B steps in; cycle alternates. Run 3-4 minutes per set or to a target count of clean passes.

## Coaching points
- One passer at a time in the seam — no helpers from the line; the test is owning the seam alone.
- Toss/serve tempo stays honest so each passer reads, moves, platforms.
- Target grades aloud so the passer hears feedback before shagging.
- Between reps, passer walks through the miss with an intentional shadow move instead of chatting in line.

## Variations
- **Add a server:** replace tosses with a live server from a box, attacking line-specific seams.
- **Reading cue:** coach varies shoulder angle pre-contact; passers call "short" or "deep" before platforming.
- **Competitive count:** first line to 10 clean 3-passes wins; shanks reset.

## Adaptations by level
- **14U:** tosses only, 5 m pass distance, simple "mine" call before each rep.
- **HS:** mix tosses and float serves from 9 m behind the endline, 2 m target window.
- **College:** full-distance jump-float/topspin, 1 m target window, live setter receiving the ball.
