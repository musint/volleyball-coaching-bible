# Munciana Five and Five Setting (Set and Cover)

## Setup
Two parallel groups across one court. Group 1: left-back DS sets to right-front hitter. Group 2: right-back DS sets to left-front hitter. Sets must arrive **5 feet off the net and 5 feet inside the antenna** — the "five and five" — so the hitter has both height and a clear visual on the block. Coach tosses the first ball as a bad pass.

## Execution
1. Coach tosses an "off" ball (away from the setter).
2. The DS catches/sets a 5-foot-off, 5-foot-in ball to the front-row hitter.
3. Hitter approaches and hits; the DS-setter and any teammates around her run **cover** to put hands on the floor under the hitter before the ball is caught/landed.
4. **[00:34:19]** "When we cover, we put our hands on the floor to signify we're there. If the hands are late, that means we didn't get where we need to go."
5. Run ~90 seconds, then switch sides so every athlete sets both left and right.

## Coaching points
- The set has to be high enough that cover gets there. **[00:34:14]** "The cover guy wants to be there by the time the ball is caught. Otherwise you're not setting high enough."
- "Set it five off, five in." Five off the net keeps non-setter setters out of the over-set danger; five in keeps the hitter swinging with full geometry.
- Out-of-system framing: **[00:35:40]** "You need to have a plan because you're going to be out of system about 80 percent of the time."
- Every player runs through this drill — every player handles bad balls, not just the setter.

## Variations
- Coach toss replaced by a partner-tossed shanked ball to add pursuit before the set.
- Add a blocker to the front row so the hitter learns to swing past or tool a 5x5 ball against a real arm.
- Live: chop a ball over the net rather than tossing — turns the drill into [[munciana-mia-drill]].

## Adaptations by level
- **14U**: drop the cover requirement; focus on landing the set 5x5 with hands.
- **HS**: full set-and-cover; require hands-on-floor by the time the hitter contacts.
- **College**: add a digger and play out the rally; first ball still must be a 5x5 set.
