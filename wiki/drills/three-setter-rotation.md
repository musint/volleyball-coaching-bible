# Three-Setter Rotation

## Setup

Three setting stations on one side of the net, each with a designated target zone and a target catcher (or hoop):

- **Station A — High-4:** setter at Zone 2/3, delivers a high outside set to Zone 4.
- **Station B — Middle 31:** setter at Zone 3, delivers a quick 31 (in front of the setter, between Zone 3 and Zone 4) to a middle.
- **Station C — Back-2:** setter at Zone 2/3, back-sets to the Zone 2 antenna.

Three setters occupy the three stations. A coach at mid-court feeds each with a clean in-system pass. Remaining players shag or catch at the targets. Station rotation every 2 minutes so every setter gets reps at every zone.

## Execution

Coach feeds station A, B, C in sequence. Each setter delivers the called set for her station and returns to ready position for the next rep. Five contacts per station before the coach resets the feeding line. After 2 minutes, setters rotate one station clockwise. Full cycle — three stations times three setters — is 12 minutes.

## Coaching points

- Every setter gets matched reps at every zone — the rotation is the whole point. A program that only works the starting setter on her best zone does not develop bench depth.
- Same hand mechanics across all three stations. The 31 is not a different technique from the high-4; it is the same hand-hinge with a different release height and speed.
- On the back-2, check that shoulders are still square to the net at contact.
- Liskevych-style cue: systematic skill-by-skill work, hand position and footwork first, target alignment second, decision-making third. Do not skip the hand shape check to get to more reps.

## Variations

- Add a jump-set constraint on the 31 station to train the middle-timing jump-set.
- Live attack at each target instead of a catcher.
- Random station calls — coach calls A, B, or C on each rep — to load the setter's decision.

## Adaptations by level

- **14U:** Two stations only (high-4 and back-2); drop the 31. Coach-tossed feed.
- **HS:** Full three stations; live pass from a passer at half the reps.
- **College:** Live passers, live attack, film review of disguise across all three zones.
