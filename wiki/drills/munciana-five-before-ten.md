# Munciana Five Before Ten

## Setup
Servers behind one endline with a ball cart. Receiving side runs a no-pass shaggers' rotation; balls go straight back to the cart. Coach posts running counts of two numbers per server (or per team): **lets** and **errors**. A "let" is a serve that hits the top of the net tape and falls in.

## Execution
1. Servers cycle one at a time. Each serve lands in one of three buckets:
   - **Let serve (hits tape, lands in)**: counts toward 5.
   - **Error (net or out)**: counts toward 10.
   - **Clean in-bounds serve**: no count either way.
2. Goal: get **five let serves before accumulating ten errors**. **[00:31:25]** "Five let serves before you accumulate ten errors."
3. Servers must aim the serve **between the top of the tape and the second red** on the net's vertical band — i.e., barely over. **[00:31:32]**
4. Block ends when 5 lets are reached (drill cleared) or 10 errors are reached (drill failed).

## Coaching points
- This is the aggressive-mentality drill. **[00:31:48]** "I want that aggressive serving mentality and you need to practice this and you're going to harp on errors. You want to harp on these guys being aggressive."
- Two ways to create speed on a serve: **[00:32:00]** "One is to serve the ball really hard. The other is to serve the ball really low." Five-before-ten rewards the second one explicitly.
- Every server should look at the top of the tape and the second red **before** every toss as a barometer for trajectory.
- Errors are not failure here — they are evidence the server is aiming low enough to score points. A server that hits 100% in is too safe.

## Variations
- Two-team race: which side hits 5 lets first.
- Reverse the bar: 3 lets before 6 errors (tighter; 18U / college level).
- Combine with [[munciana-no-gut-serving]]: lets only count if they also avoid the gut.

## Adaptations by level
- **14U**: not appropriate as written — 14U servers don't have the consistency to chase the tape. Run [[serve-targets]] or [[munciana-5-6-seam-noodle]] instead.
- **HS**: standard 5 before 10, full team rotation.
- **College**: 3 before 6 with all six receive zones live (a let must also land in-bounds anywhere).
