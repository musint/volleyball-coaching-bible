# USA Volleyball Coach Academy

## Platform Structure

The Coach Academy represents "a new way of learning" for volleyball coaches. Rather than consolidating content in one location, USA Volleyball has shifted toward "a multi-platform ecosystem to create more engaging and accessible content."

## Access and Membership

Members can access the Coach Academy through SportsEngine by logging in and launching the "USA Volleyball Academy" banner. The platform uses Litmos as its learning management system. Non-members can purchase access to the NTDP Academy separately.

**Browser requirement:** Google Chrome is the preferred browser; Internet Explorer is not supported.

## Curriculum Organization

The academy organizes coursework into three achievement tiers:

### Bronze Tier
Foundational content across five categories:
- **Craft:** Fundamental skills, team systems, and coaching experience
- **Body:** Health, safety, nutrition, and motor learning basics
- **Mind:** Mental training and sport psychology introduction
- **Heart:** Parent communication, inclusion, and athlete wellness
- **Team:** Legal responsibilities and team culture

### Silver Tier
Intermediate technical instruction featuring detailed modules on:
- Serve receive mechanics with national team staff
- Out-of-system beach setting progressions
- Transition play and middle positioning
- Motor learning frameworks

### Gold Tier
Advanced live sessions covering specialized topics like defensive systems, practice design optimization, and world championship analysis, led by elite coaches and U.S. National Team staff.

## Content Focus Areas

Courses emphasize practical applications: drill progressions, scouting strategies, athlete development planning, and coaching philosophy grounded in sport science principles.

## Supporting Organizations

USA Volleyball partners with the U.S. Olympic & Paralympic Committee, SafeSport, and regional volleyball associations to develop curriculum.
