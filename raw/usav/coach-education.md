# Coach Education - USA Volleyball

## Overview

USA Volleyball's Coach Education program aims to "empower and inspire volleyball coaches at all levels through innovative, comprehensive and athlete-centered educational" offerings. The organization emphasizes developing lifelong learners who can positively impact athletes both competitively and personally.

## Required Eligibility Requirements

Member coaches must complete three mandatory items to participate in sanctioned events:

- **IMPACT** training (online or in-person format)
- **SafeSport Training**
- **Background Check**

These requirements are processed through the USA Volleyball Member Management System (SportsEngine).

## IMPACT Training

IMPACT represents the baseline coaching education requirement for USA Volleyball junior club coaches. The course takes approximately four hours and allows participants to pause and resume. Key features include:

- Available on-demand through USAV Academy
- Free for USA Volleyball members
- Includes e-book and PDF manual formats
- Does not include SafeSport (completed separately)

The organization notes that while renewal is not currently mandated nationally, individual regions or clubs may impose stricter standards.

## SafeSport and Background Screening

SafeSport training is mandatory before coaches interact with athletes. The program is "free for USA Volleyball members who take the training through their SportsEngine account." All member coaches must also successfully pass a background screen.

## Coach Academy

The USA Volleyball Coach Academy provides an interactive online platform featuring curated content aligned with the USA Volleyball Development Model. The platform includes:

- Learning paths and modules
- Monthly announcements of new content
- Live sessions with national team coaches and experts
- Resources for season planning and skill development

## Clinics and Workshops

USA Volleyball supports in-person educational opportunities through:

- **USAV National Faculty** providing nationwide workshops
- **Regional Instructors** delivering localized training within their areas
- Customizable modules adapted to organizational needs

## Education FAQ Highlights

The organization clarified that while Coaching Acceleration Program (CAP) certifications have been discontinued, previous achievements "will be recognized and be part of the new point, badge and leaderboard system."

The Coach Academy operates separately from eligibility requirements, requiring coaches to create independent profiles rather than accessing it exclusively through SportsEngine.
