# Coach Tools - USA Volleyball

## Overview

This resource page provides volleyball coaches with drills, competition ideas, and educational materials to support their coaching development.

## Main Content Sections

### Drills and Competition Ideas

The page features three categories of drills:

**Indoor Drills** - Including submissions from coaches nationwide such as:
- At-home Training with Kami Miner
- Setter Drills for Trainer
- Exchange, Hawaii Defend and Set, and Hitter vs Hitter Tic-Tac-Toe

**Beach Drills** - Community-contributed exercises including:
- Over Under Passer Hitter
- Dig 2, X-Box Competition
- 5-5-5 Poke-Shot-Swing

**Advanced Indoor Drills** - More sophisticated techniques like:
- Fake Out, Pick Points
- Read the Hitter, Ultimate Mintonette

### Competition Ideas

The resource includes various competition formats such as Pools of Three, Round Robin All-Court, and the Competitive Cauldron with accompanying spreadsheet tools.

### Educational Resources

**Growing the Game** - John Kessel's coaching blog compiled into four PDF volumes covering 2008-2015, plus webinars on contact maximization and motor learning principles.

**Minivolley Book** - Available in English, Spanish, and Italian.

## Featured Resources

The page highlights recent coaching articles addressing goal-setting, team culture, athlete development, and leadership strategies for coaches navigating change and providing effective feedback.
