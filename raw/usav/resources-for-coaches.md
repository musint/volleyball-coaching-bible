# USA Volleyball Resources for Coaches

## Main Heading
"You can make a difference in the life of an athlete."

The page emphasizes that coaches—whether PE teachers, high school, club, or college level—serve as foundational mentors. USA Volleyball provides "a wealth of resources to improve your teaching and expand your coaching skills, including articles, videos, drills and tools."

## Key Resources Section

**USA Volleyball Coach Academy**
An online interactive learning platform featuring curated content aligned with the USA Volleyball Development Model, organized by learning paths, courses, and modules.

**Member Coach Requirements**
The IMPACT course is mandated for all USA Volleyball junior club coaches, alongside SafeSport training completion before athlete interaction.

**Coach Tools**
Compiled materials including drills, competition ideas, blogs, book recommendations, and webinars designed to enhance coaching knowledge and build success foundations.

**Lesson Plans**
Age-appropriate curricula spanning elementary through high school, emphasizing game-like, engaging instruction to develop athlete interest.

**Youth Hub: for Coaches**
Free online courses through partnership with the U.S. Olympic and Paralympic Committee, featuring Connection Based Coaching, How to Coach Kids, and Coaching Girls—all part of the Million Coaches Challenge.

**Growing the Game Blog**
Articles by John Kessel, USA Volleyball's director of sport development, offering coaching experience and court instruction guidance.

**Video Library**
Resources featuring club and national team matches, inspiring narratives, skill development tips, animated drills, and expert advice.

## Recent Coach Resources

Featured articles address college recruiting preparation, coach self-care practices, shoulder injury prevention, concussion awareness, and building athlete buy-in.
