# Chasing Greatness by Karch Kiraly (AOC store page)

## Description (as listed on AOC store)

A self-improvement guide by the head coach of the U.S. women's 2021 Olympic gold medal team and three-time Olympic gold medalist. The book presents methods for personal advancement in athletics and life, emphasizing purposeful practice, adversity management, willpower development, and team dynamics.

## Target audience

Coaches, players, business professionals, parents, and anyone seeking self-improvement across various pursuits.
